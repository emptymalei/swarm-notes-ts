# Skewness Dispersion

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

The cross-sectional variance of firm-level realized skewness, used as an aggregate predictor for future market returns.


## Why It Matters

It introduces a specific cross-sectional moment-based signal as a robust temporal predictor, which is distinct from traditional mean/volatility-based forecasting.

## Evidence
