# False Positive Mining (FPM)

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A strategy that selectively curates adaptation samples from unlabeled test data based on anomaly scores and latent feature similarity to refine models under distribution shift.


## Why It Matters

Selective sample curation is a crucial mechanism in test-time adaptation for time-series to avoid the catastrophic forgetting of pre-trained normality features.
