# Warm-transition Holdout Training

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A training strategy that mitigates memorization by holding out frequent transitions, forcing models to learn from transferable signals rather than rote frequency patterns.


## Why It Matters

This is a distinct regularization strategy that shifts focus from memorizing high-frequency events to learning generalizable compositional features, which is highly reusable in other long-tail forecasting tasks.

## Evidence

> It further uses warm-transition holdout training to discourage memorization of frequent transitions and encourage generalization from transferable signals.
