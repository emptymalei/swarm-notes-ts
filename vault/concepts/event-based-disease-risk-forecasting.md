# Event-based Disease Risk Forecasting

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A forecasting paradigm that predicts the onset of risk periods rather than status to avoid persistence bias and provide actionable early warnings.


## Why It Matters

Shifts the paradigm from standard binary classification of daily states to event detection to mitigate persistence bias in disease warning systems, favoring actionable lead times.
