# Semi-relativistic Boris Correction for MHD

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A numerical stability technique that utilizes semi-relativistic modifications to the Boris integrator to handle extreme magnetic field strengths without forcing prohibitively small computational time steps.


## Why It Matters

Enables high-fidelity magnetohydrodynamic simulations of solar coronal phenomena by bypassing severe stability constraints in strong-field regimes.
