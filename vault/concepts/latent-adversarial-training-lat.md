# Latent Adversarial Training (LAT)

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A training technique that hardens models by injecting adversarial perturbations directly into the residual stream to expose and neutralize latent failure modes.


## Why It Matters

Addresses the fundamental challenge of removing embedded dangerous behaviors in LLMs that standard safety training fails to catch.

## Evidence

> Latent Adversarial Training (LAT) removes dangerous behaviors by optimizing perturbations in the residual stream to elicit failure modes, then training under those perturbations.
