# Discounted Vovk-Azoury-Warmuth (VAW)

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

An ensemble-based online learning approach utilizing a geometric grid of discount factors for dynamic regret minimization.


## Why It Matters

It serves as the core algorithmic mechanism for achieving dynamic regret bounds in an infinite-dimensional RKHS setting.

## Evidence

> The proposed method transfers the finite-dimensional discounted Vovk--Azoury--Warmuth approach of Jacobsen & Cutkosky (2024) to the RKHS setting by means of finite-dimensional subspace approximations.
