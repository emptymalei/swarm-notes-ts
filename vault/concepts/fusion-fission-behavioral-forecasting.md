# Fusion-fission behavioral forecasting

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A framework for forecasting undesirable AI behavioral shifts by modeling the dynamics of response categories as competing basins in a vector-generalized fusion-fission model.


## Why It Matters

The paper proposes a novel, architecture-agnostic mechanism borrowed from active matter physics to predict AI behavioral shifts by modeling response trajectories as competing basins.

## Evidence

> a vector generalization of fusion-fission group dynamics observed in living and active-matter systems drives -- and can forecast -- future shifts in the AI's behavior
