# Latent Distribution Matching (LDM)

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A theoretical framework that casts self-supervised learning as the dual optimization of alignment with a latent model and maximization of latent entropy to prevent collapse.


## Why It Matters

Provides a unifying theoretical framework for diverse SSL methodologies, encompassing contrastive, non-contrastive, and predictive approaches.

## Evidence

> We cast SSL as latent distribution matching (LDM): learning representations that maximize their log-probability under an assumed latent model (alignment), while maximizing latent entropy to prevent collapse (uniformity).
