# Tree-of-Evidence (ToE)

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

An inference-time beam search algorithm that identifies minimal, discrete subsets of multimodal evidence required to reproduce a Large Multimodal Model's prediction.


## Why It Matters

Provides a novel search-based approach to multimodal interpretability that treats evidence selection as a discrete optimization problem rather than relying on soft-attention weights.

## Evidence

> ToE employs lightweight Evidence Bottlenecks that score coarse groups or units of data... and performs a beam search to identify the compact evidence set required to reproduce the model's prediction.
