# Four-gate compliance pathway

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A systematic analytical framework for identifying and resolving institutional barriers in the integration of distributed energy resources into resource adequacy programs.


## Why It Matters

It provides a novel, unified framework to systematically analyze and diagnose institutional friction points in integrating distributed energy resources into electricity markets.

## Evidence

> This paper introduces a four-gate compliance pathway (entry and classification, metering and verification, accreditation, and enforcement), preceded by an upstream forecasting layer, as a unified lens for tracing where DER capacity value is lost at the institutional interfaces between these stages.
