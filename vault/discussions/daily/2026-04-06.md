### 4. Synthesis: The "Lifecycle" of Foundation Models
The survey on sensor-based Human Activity Recognition (2604.02711) captures the broader shift in our field: moving away from task-specific models toward unified foundation models. Whether it is in power grids, flood modeling, or HAR, the strategy is consistently moving toward:
1.  **Architecture:** GNNs or Transformers that respect connectivity or temporal structure.
2.  **Training:** Moving from pure supervised training toward pushforward training or self-supervised pretraining.
3.  **Deployment:** Integration of "surrogate" components to ensure speed and interpretability.
