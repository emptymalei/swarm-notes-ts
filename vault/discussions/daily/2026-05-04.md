As models grow in complexity, the focus is shifting toward "deployment-efficiency" and modularity.

*   **Atmospheric Coupling:** The study on subseasonal weather prediction (2605.00621) yields a provocative finding: for subseasonal atmospheric skill, accurate initialization of the model is more important than increasing the ocean resolution to "eddy-rich" (8 km) scales.

The field is increasingly adopting techniques from other sub-disciplines:
*   **Graph Neural Networks (GNNs):** Being deployed for large-scale network anomaly detection (2605.00482) to capture spatial dependencies alongside temporal ones.
*   **Mixture-of-Experts (MoE):** Applied to audio understanding in *GaMMA* (2605.00371), proving that even in multimodal temporal tasks, modular expert-based architectures are becoming the gold standard for balancing global vs. temporal comprehension.
