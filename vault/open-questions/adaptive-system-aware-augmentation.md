title: Adaptive System-Aware Augmentation
---

**Background:** Data augmentation for complex, multivariate clinical time series often relies on predefined clinical system partitions that may not capture latent or cross-system dependencies.

**Question / Future Work:** Exploring adaptive methods for discovering latent system-aware dependencies and dynamic augmentation strategies is necessary to improve the clinical plausibility of generated samples in complex physiological models.

**Why It Matters:** Capturing dynamic physiological dependencies is essential for generating robust samples that correctly reflect the evolving interactions between clinical systems.
