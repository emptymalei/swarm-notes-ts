title: Self-supervised Foundation Models for Astronomy
---

**Background:** Stellar light curve classification using deep learning architectures often relies on supervised learning with limited labeled datasets, which can lead to overfitting and difficulties in generalizing to new, unseen astrophysical data.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work aims to transition from supervised learning to self-supervised learning schemes that can effectively utilize vast amounts of unlabeled stellar light curve data to build foundation models.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing the dependence on labeled data is a fundamental bottleneck in scaling machine learning models to the millions of light curves produced by modern space missions.
