title: PINN Finance Generalization Bottlenecks
---

**Background:** Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) integrate mathematical or financial laws into the training process as structural constraints. While successful in physical domains, their application to finance remains theoretically and empirically nascent.

**Question / Future Work:** Addressing the foundational challenge of model generalization, data scarcity, and the technical requirement of mapping non-differentiable or high-frequency market dynamics into valid, differentiable physics-informed constraints for portfolio management.

**Why It Matters:** It is a core technical bottleneck to applying the PINN paradigm to finance without overfitting or violating structural economic assumptions.
