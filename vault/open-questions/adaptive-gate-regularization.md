title: Adaptive Gate Regularization
---

**Background:** Gate regularization hyperparameters in neural network architectures often require manual tuning to balance model sparsity and predictive performance.

**Question / Future Work:** The selection of regularization coefficients for gated neural architectures, which determines the sparsity-accuracy trade-off, is often heuristic. Developing a robust, automated method for these hyperparameters remains an open challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Automated gate regulation would facilitate the scaling of gated architectures across diverse forecasting domains without the need for exhaustive manual validation.
