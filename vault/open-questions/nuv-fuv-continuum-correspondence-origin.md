title: NUV/FUV continuum correspondence origin
---

**Background:** NUV and FUV continuum enhancements are observed to correspond spatially and temporally in solar flares. The mechanisms driving this relationship remain to be fully understood.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the physical origins of the observed spatial and temporal correspondence between NUV and FUV continuum enhancements in solar flares to determine if they share a common physical mechanism or are driven by distinct but coupled processes within the solar chromosphere.

**Why It Matters:** This correspondence is a critical diagnostic tool; understanding it is essential for refining radiative hydrodynamic flare models.
