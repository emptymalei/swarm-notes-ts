title: Hybrid LLM-Physics Control Architectures
---

**Background:** Large language models are increasingly being integrated into safety-critical embodied AI systems, yet a persistent tension exists between their semantic reasoning capabilities and the numerical precision required for reliable physical control.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating architectural paradigms that effectively fuse high-level semantic reasoning from LLMs with the specialized numerical precision of traditional forecasters to bridge the gap between intent-driven control and safety-constrained physical prediction.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the fundamental 'Precision-Controllability Dichotomy' and 'Dynamic Complexity Gap' identified in safety-critical embodied AI, which are central barriers to deploying LLMs in physical environments.
