title: Cross-Center Generalization Challenges
---

**Background:** Clinical predictive models frequently exhibit performance degradation when deployed in environments with different charting protocols and patient demographics than those in the training set.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing robust methodologies for cross-center domain adaptation and validation to mitigate performance drops caused by institutional practice heterogeneity remains an open challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Essential for clinical safety and broad adoption of any predictive model in real-world healthcare settings.
