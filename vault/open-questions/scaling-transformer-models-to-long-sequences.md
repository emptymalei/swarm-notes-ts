title: Scalability to long sequences
---

**Background:** Processing high-cadence or long-duration time-series data in Transformers often results in increased computational complexity and challenges in capturing long-range dependencies over extended temporal sequences.

**Question / Future Work:** Evaluating the impact of sequence length on model efficiency and investigating optimal adaptive windowing or downsampling techniques that preserve variability features in long light curves.

**Why It Matters:** Efficiently handling longer temporal sequences is crucial for maximizing scientific throughput in future high-cadence space missions.

**Evidence:** it is possible that the longer sequences make it more difficult for the model to learn long-range dependencies and increase computational costs.
