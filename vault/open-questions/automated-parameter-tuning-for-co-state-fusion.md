title: Automated Parameter Tuning
---

**Background:** The co-state fusion framework relies on hyperparameters to balance measurement compatibility and observation noise that currently require manual, mission-specific calibration.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing automated methods for setting regularization and weighting parameters is necessary to achieve true autonomy and ensure the framework remains robust across diverse, high-stakes operational environments.

**Why It Matters:** Reducing manual calibration is critical for deploying adaptive anomaly detection in complex, long-duration autonomous systems.
