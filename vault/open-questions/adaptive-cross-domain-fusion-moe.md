title: Adaptive Cross-Domain MoE Fusion
---

**Background:** Mixture-of-experts architectures in time series models currently rely on unified routing, but the optimal coordination between temporally-aligned heterogeneous domains remains underexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** Develop adaptive fusion mechanisms for cross-domain representations (time vs. frequency) in MoE architectures that dynamically weight experts based on the local importance of specific temporal frequencies or regimes.

**Why It Matters:** Improving the fusion of heterogeneous representations is essential for handling multi-scale temporal data without information misalignment.
