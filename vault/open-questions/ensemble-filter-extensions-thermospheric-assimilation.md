title: Ensemble Filter Extensions
---

**Background:** State estimation in non-linear, reduced-order thermospheric models often assumes Gaussian error propagation and employs linearization-based filters like the Extended Kalman Filter.

**Question / Future Work:** Explore ensemble-based or sampling-based filtering techniques for latent-space assimilation to more accurately represent non-Gaussian forecast uncertainties and to better account for the nonlinearities in the relationship between the latent state and physical measurements.

**Why It Matters:** Replacing linearization-based filters with ensemble-based or sampling-based alternatives can improve performance in highly nonlinear dynamical regimes typical of severe space weather events.
