title: Improving VLM Temporal Reasoning
---

**Background:** Existing research indicates that off-the-shelf VLMs, even when prompted effectively, struggle with complex temporal reasoning and long-horizon stock price forecasting.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should explore whether fine-tuning or specialized task-specific training can enhance the temporal reasoning and predictive accuracy of VLMs when interpreting multi-scale financial candlestick charts, as current prompt-based methods show limited effectiveness for long-term forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** It is necessary to determine if the identified performance bottleneck in long-term temporal reasoning is an inherent limitation of VLM architectures or a result of insufficient task-specific domain alignment.
