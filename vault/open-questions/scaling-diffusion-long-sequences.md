title: Scaling Diffusion for Sequences
---

**Background:** Diffusion models for time-series data often struggle with high-dimensional or long-duration sequences due to the computational cost of iterative denoising.

**Question / Future Work:** It is necessary to investigate methods for scaling diffusion-based generative models to longer time-series sequences without incurring prohibitive computational costs, while maintaining temporal coherence over extended intervals.

**Why It Matters:** Scalability is a critical bottleneck for deploying diffusion models in real-world time-series applications where long-range dependencies are essential.

**Evidence:** Future work will explore scaling to longer sequences and integrating stronger temporal architectures.
