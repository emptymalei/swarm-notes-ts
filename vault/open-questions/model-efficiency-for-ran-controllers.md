title: Efficient Model Deployment Methods
---

**Background:** Deploying machine learning models within O-RAN Near-RT RICs necessitates strict adherence to computational and memory constraints. Although lightweight architectures exist, further reduction in resource usage is often required for broader deployment on diverse hardware platforms.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to explore advanced techniques such as model quantization, pruning, and hardware-aware Neural Architecture Search (NAS) to minimize the memory footprint of CSI prediction models, facilitating their execution on resource-constrained RAN Intelligent Controller platforms.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for the practical, large-scale deployment of AI-based channel prediction in resource-constrained edge computing environments.
