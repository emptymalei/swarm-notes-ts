title: Stochastic Liquid Attention Networks
---

**Background:** Stochastic differential equations (SDEs) extend deterministic ODE models by incorporating random processes, offering a framework for uncertainty quantification in dynamical systems.

**Question / Future Work:** Transitioning from deterministic ODE-based attention, as seen in the LAN mechanism, to stochastic differential equations (SDEs) could provide a natural approach to quantifying the intrinsic uncertainty within the attention mechanism.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing uncertainty in attention is critical for more robust and reliable sequence modeling, especially in high-stakes control environments.

**Evidence:** Replacing the LAN-ODE with stochastic differential equation (SDE) is a natural direction for future work, as it will introduce intrinsic uncertainty quantification into the attention mechanism.
