title: Support for heterogeneous workloads
---

**Background:** Modern workload offloading frameworks in computing continua often require extensions to accommodate application-specific quality-of-service (QoS) requirements and diverse resource profiles.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending workload offloading frameworks to handle heterogeneous application classes, such as varying latency sensitivity, reliability requirements, or computational resource demands, is necessary to improve the practical applicability of these systems to real-world, multi-tenant IoT ecosystems.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for moving from theoretical workload models to functional deployments that must satisfy diverse user-level SLAs.
