title: Standardizing emerging diabetes data streams
---

**Background:** Machine learning for diabetes management requires integrating increasingly diverse physiological data streams from sensors and insulin delivery systems.

**Question / Future Work:** As novel biosensors and pharmacological interventions (e.g., continuous ketone monitors, new GLP-1 agonists) become common in clinical datasets, establishing community-wide standards for their metadata, unit conventions, and temporal synchronization is critical to prevent future data fragmentation. Addressing this requires defining formal extensibility protocols that ensure new data streams remain interoperable with established glucose-insulin modeling frameworks.

**Why It Matters:** Proactive standardization of emerging modalities is essential to maintain the long-term utility and interoperability of clinical datasets for holistic physiological modeling.
