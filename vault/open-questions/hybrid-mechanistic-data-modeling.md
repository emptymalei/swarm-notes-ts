title: Hybrid Mechanistic-Data Modeling
---

**Background:** Mechanistic process models encode biochemical domain knowledge but are difficult to calibrate, whereas purely data-driven models lack such physical constraints.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing methodologies to integrate learned process dynamics with mechanistic constraints (e.g., mass balance or biochemical pathways) is necessary for improving robustness and interpretability in critical industrial digital twins.

**Why It Matters:** Hybrid modeling is vital for increasing regulatory acceptance and long-term reliability in infrastructure monitoring systems.
