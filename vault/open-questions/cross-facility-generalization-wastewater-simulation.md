title: Generalization Across Wastewater Facilities
---

**Background:** Wastewater treatment plants operate across multiple sites with varying configurations, sensor suites, and biological processes, yet current data-driven simulators are typically validated on data from a single facility.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating the generalizability and transferability of learned simulators across different wastewater treatment facilities with diverse biological configurations and sensor suites remains an open research challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization is the primary barrier to the broad industrial adoption of data-driven digital twin simulators across diverse infrastructure sites.
