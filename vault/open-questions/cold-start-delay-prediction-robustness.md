title: Cold-start delay prediction robustness
---

**Background:** Logistics networks frequently encounter cold-start scenarios where new nodes or shipping lanes lack sufficient historical volume to establish baseline performance metrics.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop predictive methods for delivery delays that remain robust and sensitive to routing degradation even when historical data per node is sparse or entirely absent.

**Why It Matters:** Maintaining operational reliability in the presence of data scarcity at individual nodes is a critical challenge for industrial supply chain forecasting.
