title: Generalization of Frequency Degradation
---

**Background:** Temporal foundation models based on patch-based architectures often exhibit performance degradation at specific frequency harmonics aligned with their internal patch-stride configuration.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains unclear whether the observed harmonic frequency-domain degradation in patch-based foundation models is an inherent, unavoidable architectural limitation or a phenomenon that can be mitigated through training strategies or architectural adaptation. Identifying if these artifacts persist in non-stationary real-world signals is critical for reliability.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding these limitations is essential for deploying foundation models in signal processing and information fusion where frequency-domain fidelity is critical.
