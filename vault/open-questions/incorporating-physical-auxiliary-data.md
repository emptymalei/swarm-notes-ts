title: Incorporating physical auxiliary data
---

**Background:** Urban Land Surface Temperature (LST) is influenced by complex local factors, including land cover, urban geometry, and anthropogenic heat, which are often only implicitly encoded in satellite observations.

**Question / Future Work:** Evaluate the impact of explicitly integrating auxiliary physical variables—such as vegetation indices or urban morphology metrics—into deep learning architectures, balancing the gains in spatial precision against risks of city-specific bias and increased complexity.

**Why It Matters:** Explicitly modeling these factors could lead to more physically accurate and reliable urban climate forecasting compared to models relying solely on statistical inference from LST signals.
