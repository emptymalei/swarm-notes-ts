title: Faithfulness vs. Causal Discovery
---

**Background:** Mechanistic interpretability tools, such as Temporal Functional Circuits, allow for interventional verification of model-internal dependencies in KANs.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains unclear how model-internal faithfulness, such as edge-level interventions, maps to true causal discovery in time-series dynamics, and whether these explanations reliably reflect causal processes in non-stationary environments.

**Why It Matters:** Distinguishing between predictive faithfulness and true causal explanation is critical for high-stakes forecasting.
