title: Conformal Validity for Markovian Data
---

**Background:** Conformal prediction typically relies on the assumption of exchangeable data, which is rarely met in temporally-dependent systems like Markov processes.

**Question / Future Work:** Formalizing conditions under which conformal prediction guarantees hold for non-exchangeable Markovian structures remains a fundamental theoretical and practical bottleneck.

**Why It Matters:** Validating the application of conformal inference to dependent data is essential for reliable uncertainty quantification in high-stakes time-series forecasting.
