title: Adaptive Social Distance Priors
---

**Background:** Socially compliant navigation is currently governed by proximity-based loss functions that treat social distancing as a fixed distance constraint.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a fundamental lack of adaptive, context-aware social distance modeling that can account for the nuance and diversity of human-robot social norms in complex indoor environments.

**Why It Matters:** Static distance constraints fail to generalize across diverse social contexts, necessitating development of dynamic priors conditioned on high-dimensional semantic human behavior.
