title: Theoretical certification of co-state monitoring
---

**Background:** Autonomous navigation and state estimation systems often fail to distinguish between stochastic sensor noise and fundamental internal model-measurement inconsistencies, which complicates early hazard detection.

**Question / Future Work:** Formalizing the theoretical guarantees (detectability, observability, and mathematical robustness) of co-state-based consistency monitoring is essential to transition from empirical performance to safety-certified operational reliability.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing formal theoretical guarantees is a prerequisite for the safety certification of autonomous navigation systems in mission-critical applications.
