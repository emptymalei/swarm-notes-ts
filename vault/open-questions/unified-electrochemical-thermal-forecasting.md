title: Unified electrochemical-thermal forecasting
---

**Background:** Thermal runaway events in electrochemical systems are driven by the intricate coupling between chemical kinetics and heat generation pathways.

**Question / Future Work:** There is no established framework for integrating electrochemical kinetic models with thermal dynamics within a unified, physics-informed learning architecture. Addressing this requires managing the disparate temporal scales and computational complexities of these two distinct physical processes.

**Why It Matters:** Decoupling chemical reactions from thermal diffusion limits the predictive accuracy and lead time of safety-critical prognostic models.
