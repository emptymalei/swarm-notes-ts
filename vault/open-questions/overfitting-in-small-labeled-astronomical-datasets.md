title: Overfitting in astronomical classification
---

**Background:** Transformer-based models often face challenges with overfitting when applied to datasets with limited labeled examples, a common scenario in scientific time-series classification.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing effective regularization strategies or self-supervised learning paradigms to mitigate overfitting in transformer-based classifiers when working with sparse labeled astronomical time-series datasets.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling classification accuracy without proportional increases in human-labeled data is a fundamental bottleneck in high-throughput astronomical surveys.

**Evidence:** the model remains susceptible to overfitting due to the relatively small size of our labeled training set.
