title: ADMM penalty parameter selection
---

**Background:** The penalty parameter in the alternating direction method of multipliers (ADMM) governs the convergence rate and numerical conditioning of the subproblems, yet there is no universal theoretical method for its selection.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to develop robust, adaptive, or automated strategies for selecting the penalty parameter in ADMM-based numerical solvers, as current approaches typically rely on heuristics.

**Why It Matters:** The choice of the penalty parameter significantly affects both the numerical stability and the convergence speed of the algorithm, which is critical for real-time applications where stringent computation time constraints exist.
