title: Asymptotic properties of FLOC estimators
---

**Background:** Cyclostationary time series analysis has historically relied on the autocovariance function, which is mathematically invalid for heavy-tailed processes with infinite variance. The fractional lower-order covariance (FLOC) provides an alternative framework for defining cyclostationarity and dependence measures in such infinite-variance settings.

**Question / Future Work:** Formal asymptotic properties (such as consistency, asymptotic distribution, and rate of convergence) for FLOC-based dependence measures like peFLOACF and peFLOPACF remain to be established, hindering rigorous statistical inference without reliance on empirical Monte Carlo simulations.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing the theoretical convergence of these estimators is crucial for developing reliable, non-heuristic tests in heavy-tailed cyclostationary modeling.
