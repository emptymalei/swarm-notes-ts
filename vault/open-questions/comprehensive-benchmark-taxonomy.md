title: Comprehensive Benchmark Taxonomy
---

**Background:** Existing time-series model evaluation frameworks often define benchmarks narrowly by forecast horizon and domain, while neglecting critical statistical properties like stationarity and seasonality.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need for a flexible taxonomy for benchmark tasks that explicitly incorporates core statistical properties and data regimes to move beyond simple aggregate leaderboard rankings.

**Why It Matters:** Standardizing benchmark taxonomies is essential for identifying the specific strengths and weaknesses of different model architectures across varying data conditions.
