title: Integrating Time-Varying Covariates
---

**Background:** Customer base models traditionally rely on static assumptions to capture latent heterogeneity, making it challenging to incorporate exogenous temporal signals.

**Question / Future Work:** The challenge lies in integrating time-varying exogenous covariates—such as calendar-based seasonality—into latent variable models without compromising the structural stability required for reliable long-term forecasting. Identifying the point of diminishing returns where model complexity for time-varying inputs undermines the robustness of the core process-based likelihood is a critical unresolved research direction.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a fundamental tension between capturing complex, external temporal dynamics and maintaining the reliable, structure-aware forecasting performance essential for high-stakes business decision-making.
