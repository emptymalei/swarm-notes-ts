title: Automated SRF Regime Discovery
---

**Background:** Superconducting radio frequency (SRF) cavities utilize high-quality factor mechanical resonators whose modal behavior is subject to transient environmental fluctuations. Fixed-order linear models often struggle to adapt to regime shifts in modal composition during active operation.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of automated techniques for discovering and updating dynamical regimes in real-time is necessary to enable estimators to adapt to shifts in modal composition without requiring structural manual intervention.

**Why It Matters:** Automated regime discovery is critical for the robustness of data-driven estimators in complex, nonstationary operational environments.
