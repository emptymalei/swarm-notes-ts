title: General Non-Gaussianity SIGW Modeling
---

**Background:** Primordial non-Gaussianity (NG) influences the amplitude and shape of the scalar-induced gravitational wave (SIGW) spectrum through higher-order correlators of primordial scalar perturbations.

**Question / Future Work:** Current studies often rely on simplified local-type NG models, leaving a need for broader non-perturbative frameworks that account for diverse inflationary non-Gaussian signatures in the SIGW spectrum.

**Why It Matters:** Simple local NG models may not capture the full phenomenology required to interpret future observational data from next-generation gravitational wave detectors.

**Evidence:** we focus on local NG alone, as this allows us to qualitatively understand its imprints and detectability. We leave a more general study for future work.
