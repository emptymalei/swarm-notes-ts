title: Extend lightweight model adaptation
---

**Background:** Forecasting models calibrated on localized urban pollution data are subject to performance degradation when deployed in environments with different emission profiles or atmospheric conditions.

**Question / Future Work:** Extend lightweight adaptation strategies, such as online residual correction, to heterogeneous datasets, different cities, and varying sensing conditions to comprehensively assess the broader applicability and transferability of the proposed forecasting approach.

**Why It Matters:** Assessing transferability is crucial for operationalizing lightweight forecasting models beyond the single city (Beijing) studied, ensuring robustness across diverse urban environments.

**Evidence:** Extending lightweight adaptation strategies to heterogeneous datasets, cities, and sensing conditions will be important for assessing the broader applicability of the proposed approach.
