title: Expressiveness of Markovian Gaussian Processes
---

**Background:** Markovian Gaussian processes leverage an equivalence between certain Gaussian processes and stochastic differential equations, allowing for linear-time inference through Kalman-based methods.

**Question / Future Work:** It is not yet fully understood how expressive the class of stationary Gaussian processes representable as linear time-invariant stochastic differential equations truly is, particularly regarding the ability to approximate arbitrary stationary kernels within this framework.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the limits of this representational power is essential for determining the scope of linear-time sequential inference for arbitrary stationary kernel functions.

**Evidence:** A natural question is: how expressive are the GPs that can be represented in this way?
