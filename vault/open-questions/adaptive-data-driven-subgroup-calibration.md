title: Adaptive Data-Driven Subgroup Calibration
---

**Background:** Mondrian conformal prediction aims to provide group-specific coverage guarantees, but it often relies on predefined, static subgroup definitions that fail to capture complex intersectional vulnerabilities.

**Question / Future Work:** Future methods must move beyond fixed, a priori subgroup definitions to develop adaptive, data-driven approaches for identifying calibration cells. This involves detecting and rectifying coverage disparities across latent or emergent subgroups without the calibration-cell fragmentation that leads to instability in naive group-specific calibration.

**Why It Matters:** Moving away from manual group definitions is critical for ensuring fairness and validity guarantees are robust in diverse, real-world population data.
