title: Multivariate Extension for TSAD
---

**Background:** Most current LLM-based anomaly detection frameworks are designed for univariate time series, while many real-world industrial and monitoring scenarios involve multivariate data streams with complex inter-dependencies.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing mechanisms for cross-variable dependency analysis and diagnostic reasoning within a multi-agent framework is necessary to extend univariate TSAD systems to complex multivariate settings.

**Why It Matters:** Multivariate anomaly detection is a standard requirement in industrial and financial applications, and extending diagnostic capabilities to such settings is necessary for broader practical utility.
