title: Benchmarking Trend Detection Recall
---

**Background:** Automatic evaluation of large-scale trend detection systems is difficult due to the lack of comprehensive global ground-truth datasets for emerging topical trends.

**Question / Future Work:** The absence of a reliable, high-coverage ground-truth dataset for short-video trend detection hinders the ability to quantify system recall, leaving performance assessment primarily reliant on manual sampling or proxy metrics. Establishing a reproducible, large-scale benchmark that includes diverse, multi-platform trending events remains an unresolved challenge for the research community.

**Why It Matters:** Without a standardized ground-truth, it is impossible to objectively compare the effectiveness, recall, and sensitivity of different trend-detection architectures across disparate social media platforms.

**Evidence:** We do not report recall due to the absence of a comprehensive global ground-truth dataset.
