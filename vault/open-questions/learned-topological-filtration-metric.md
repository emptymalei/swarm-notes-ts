title: Learned Topological Filtration Metrics
---

**Background:** Topological data analysis typically relies on pre-defined metrics to build simplicial complexes or filtrations, which may not capture the most relevant structures for time-series forecasting.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an open research need for learned filtration metrics that can recover topological structures obscured by standard distance measures, thereby optimizing the topological signal for forecasting tasks.

**Why It Matters:** Determining the optimal metric for TDA is a fundamental bottleneck, as the choice of metric currently bounds the quality and expressiveness of the topological representation.
