title: Breaking EFT Lyman-alpha Degeneracies
---

**Background:** The effective field theory (EFT) of the Lyman-alpha forest provides a perturbative framework for analyzing the 1D flux power spectrum, but the projection of 3D modes along the line of sight introduces degeneracies among the numerous bias parameters.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to investigate the joint analysis of the Lyman-alpha forest with other tracers, such as quasar positions or galaxy distributions, to break these parameter degeneracies, and to further refine the treatment of higher-order bias terms.

**Why It Matters:** Breaking these degeneracies is critical for maximizing the cosmological constraining power of the Lyman-alpha forest and for moving beyond current limitations where parameter space size limits the reliability of inference.

**Evidence:** one would expect to fit a wide variety of data vectors without even modifying the fundamental cosmological parameters with such a large parameter space... This could be alleviated by including cross-correlation with matter, halos, or quasars... We leave this to future work.
