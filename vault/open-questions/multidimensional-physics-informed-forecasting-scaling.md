title: Multidimensional physics-informed forecasting scaling

**Background:** Physics-informed machine learning models often rely on simplified, single-dimensional representations of thermal diffusion to maintain computational tractability in real-time management systems.

**Question / Future Work:** It is an unresolved challenge to extend physics-informed temporal frameworks to capture complex, multidimensional heat dissipation and internal thermal gradients without sacrificing real-time computational performance. Identifying efficient ways to scale these models to spatial domains is critical for high-fidelity safety monitoring.

**Why It Matters:** Capturing spatial variations in heat distribution is essential for accurate thermal safety monitoring, yet such modeling often conflicts with real-time requirements.
