title: EnKBS Scalability in Nonlinear Systems
---

**Background:** Data assimilation methods like the EnKBS rely on linearized assumptions or ensemble-based approximations that may fail in strongly nonlinear or chaotic regimes. Understanding the limits of these approximations is essential for scientific reliability.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematic investigation is required into the accuracy, convergence, and susceptibility to degeneracy of EnKBS when applied to high-dimensional, strongly nonlinear systems. This includes developing robust mitigation strategies for non-quadratic system behaviors.

**Why It Matters:** Determining the scalability and robustness of EnKBS in strongly nonlinear scenarios is critical for its application to complex, large-scale physical modeling.
