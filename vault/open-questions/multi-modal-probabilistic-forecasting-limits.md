title: Multi-modal Probabilistic Modeling Limits
---

**Background:** Probabilistic traffic forecasting methods typically utilize standard distributions like Gaussians, which often struggle to capture the complex, multi-modal stochasticity inherent in urban traffic dynamics.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the efficacy of expressive multi-modal probability distributions when integrated into advanced neural architectures for time-series, beyond the simple GMM approach. This includes exploring how to maintain computational efficiency while improving uncertainty calibration in non-Gaussian or multi-modal scenarios.

**Why It Matters:** Essential for scaling probabilistic forecasting, as moving beyond basic distributional assumptions remains a critical challenge in spatio-temporal modeling.
