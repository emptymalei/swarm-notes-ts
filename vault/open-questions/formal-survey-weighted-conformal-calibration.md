title: Formal Survey-Weighted Conformal Calibration
---

**Background:** Conformal prediction provides distribution-free uncertainty quantification, but it currently lacks formal theoretical guarantees for survey-weighted data encountered in complex survey designs.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to develop a rigorous framework that incorporates design-based survey weights directly into the calibration process for conformal prediction. Current approaches often rely on heuristics or post-hoc adjustments, whereas a formalization is necessary to ensure finite-sample validity in population-representative inference.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for applying machine learning-based uncertainty quantification reliably in social science and policy-making where survey data are the standard for population inference.
