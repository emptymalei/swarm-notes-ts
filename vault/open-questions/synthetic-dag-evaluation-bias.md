title: Synthetic DAG evaluation bias
---

**Background:** Standard evaluations of causal discovery algorithms often use synthetic random DAGs where reachability properties can inadvertently encode the causal order.

**Question / Future Work:** How to design synthetic DAG generation procedures for causal discovery benchmarking that are robust to structural reachability heuristics and accurately represent complex causal dependencies?

**Why It Matters:** Existing synthetic benchmarks may overestimate algorithmic performance by allowing simple heuristics to recover causal order, necessitating more rigorous synthetic data generation methods.
