title: Advanced Solar Wind Thermodynamics
---

**Background:** Solar wind models often rely on simplified polytropic or isothermal assumptions, which may not capture the complex thermal energy transport and radiative processes occurring in the solar corona and interplanetary space.

**Question / Future Work:** The development and integration of more realistic thermodynamic treatments, such as incorporating conductive heating and radiative cooling, are required to improve the fidelity of background solar wind representations in global magnetohydrodynamic simulations.

**Why It Matters:** Thermodynamic realism is crucial for reproducing the structure and speed of the solar wind, which directly impacts the predicted arrival times and impact strengths of CMEs.
