title: Evaluate U-Balance across CPS domains
---

**Background:** Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) safety monitoring techniques rely on labeled datasets, which are often highly imbalanced due to the rarity of unsafe events in real-world operations.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the generalizability of the Uncertainty-Guided Label Rebalancing (U-Balance) approach across additional CPS domains, such as autonomous submarines, to assess its performance across different sensor modalities and operational settings compared to the current evaluation on Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) data.

**Why It Matters:** Assessing generalizability beyond UAVs is crucial to determine if the uncertainty-guided rebalancing mechanism is a domain-agnostic solution for CPS safety prediction under imbalance.

**Evidence:** Future work includes evaluating U-Balance across additional CPS domains, such as autonomous submarines, to assess generalisability across sensor modalities and operational settings.
