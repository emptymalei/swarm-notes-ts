title: Market behavior during oracle disputes
---

**Background:** Prediction markets leverage oracle mechanisms to resolve the outcomes of traded binary claims, and oracle dispute events can introduce significant uncertainty regarding market resolution.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to analyze the behavioral dynamics and market microstructure responses in prediction markets during oracle disputes, specifically examining how trading volume and participation respond to resolution uncertainty and the implications for information aggregation efficiency.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding market behavior during oracle disputes is critical for designing more robust decentralized resolution mechanisms and assessing the resilience of prediction markets to oracle-level adversarial or technical risks.
