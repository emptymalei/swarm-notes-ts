title: Stochastic chaotic dynamics emulation
---

**Background:** Chaotic dynamical systems often exhibit a complex interplay between deterministic chaotic evolution and intrinsic or extrinsic stochasticity. Research is needed to generalize invariant measure preservation techniques to stochastic dynamical systems.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open problem to effectively capture the complex interplay between deterministic chaotic dynamics and stochastic processes, particularly in defining and enforcing invariant statistics in the presence of noise.

**Why It Matters:** Physical systems are often inherently stochastic, and emulators must balance chaotic structural fidelity with accurate stochastic representation.

**Evidence:** Future work will also explore extending this framework to stochastic systems, where capturing the interplay between stochasticity and chaotic dynamics presents a natural and challenging generalization.
