title: Structured Prior Integration Limits
---

**Background:** The ST-PT factor graph permits the injection of symbolic temporal priors, but the performance benefits are inconsistent depending on the interplay between prior structure and signal noise.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating the conditions under which structural time-series priors provide reliable gains and developing strategies to effectively compose multiple symbolic priors within the factor graph remains a core research challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Clarifying the role of symbolic priors is essential for transitioning from purely data-driven to informed, robust time-series forecasting.

**Evidence:** The overall gain from prior modelling on real data is currently not pronounced. ... A fuller scoping of RQ1 would therefore need to combine multiple priors and to control each of these factors more carefully than the present study does.
