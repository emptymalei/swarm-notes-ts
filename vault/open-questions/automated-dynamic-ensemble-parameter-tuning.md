title: Automated Dynamic Ensemble Parameter Tuning
---

**Background:** Ensemble methods often prioritize majority class performance at the expense of minority classes in imbalanced datasets, and existing minority-aware approaches often rely on fixed parameter settings that may not generalize across different tasks or domains.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need for the development of automated, passive mechanisms to dynamically adjust ensemble parameters—such as thresholds and boosting weights—as data characteristics shift, rather than relying on manual, dataset-specific tuning. Achieving this would enhance the robustness and domain-agnostic applicability of minority-optimized ensemble frameworks.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing manual hyperparameter optimization is a significant bottleneck for scaling ensemble methods to real-world, dynamic classification tasks.
