title: Modeling Disease Progression Irreversibility
---

**Background:** Disease progression is fundamentally entropic and temporally directed, and current world models often struggle to differentiate between reversible and irreversible physiological trajectories.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to develop frameworks that incorporate the arrow of time into latent dynamics modeling, potentially by using continuous action embeddings that account for both clinical magnitude and the temporal asymmetry of pathological evolution, rather than relying on symmetric transition assumptions.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing temporal irreversibility is essential for building clinically accurate simulators that can perform valid counterfactual reasoning, which is limited by the current reliance on models that struggle with the distinction between reversible and irreversible pathologies.
