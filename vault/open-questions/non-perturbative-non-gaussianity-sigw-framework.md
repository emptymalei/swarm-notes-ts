title: Non-perturbative non-Gaussianity for SIGWs
---

**Background:** The relationship between primordial scalar perturbations and non-Gaussianity is often modeled using a simple polynomial expansion in the local non-Gaussianity parameter. While useful, this approach has limitations regarding its range of validity and its ability to capture full non-linear gravitational effects.

**Question / Future Work:** A more general, fully non-perturbative treatment of non-Gaussianity in the context of SIGW production for arbitrary equations of state is required to move beyond current perturbative limitations.

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond perturbative non-Gaussianity is essential for precise parameter estimation of the early Universe, especially for scenarios where non-linear effects may become significant or where non-local non-Gaussianity is predicted.
