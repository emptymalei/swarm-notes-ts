title: Lightweight model robustness to shifts
---

**Background:** Air quality prediction models may struggle to maintain accuracy when underlying pollution dynamics undergo sudden, significant changes, known as abrupt regime shifts.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the performance of interpretable, lightweight additive models (like Prophet variants) specifically when encountering substantial discontinuities or abrupt regime shifts in pollution patterns, which current methods are hypothesized to handle poorly.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding robustness to rare, high-impact events like abrupt regime shifts is vital for emergency planning and public safety applications of air quality forecasting.

**Evidence:** although Prophet-based approaches are generally robust to recurring temporal patterns and gradual drift, substantial discontinuities or abrupt regime shifts could still affect their stability and forecasting reliability.
