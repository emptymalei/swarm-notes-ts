title: Scaling Frequency-Domain TSFMs
---

**Background:** Time series foundation models have traditionally focused on scaling corpora in the time domain, whereas the integration of frequency-domain representations, such as wavelet-based features, remains a nascent area of exploration.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the fundamental scaling properties of frequency-domain (specifically wavelet-transformed) corpora versus time-domain corpora in TSFMs to determine if frequency-domain information provides consistent, additive benefits as model and data size grow.

**Why It Matters:** Determining the optimal data domain for scaling is critical for the development of foundation models capable of capturing both long-range trends and localized oscillatory dynamics.
