title: Closed-Loop Risk-Aware Control
---

**Background:** The current system functions primarily as an analytical and diagnostic tool for detecting internal model inconsistencies in spacecraft telemetry.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to integrate co-state-based hazard probabilities directly into active, closed-loop guidance, navigation, and control (GNC) architectures to allow for real-time, risk-aware autonomous decision-making.

**Why It Matters:** Proactive risk mitigation is a necessary step to transition from diagnostic monitoring to fully autonomous operation in uncertain environments.
