title: Continuous-Time Operator Frameworks
---

**Background:** Markov transition operators can be modeled as time-inhomogeneous processes where dynamics evolve based on external features. Continuous-time formulations are required to better address the irregular arrival of observations in high-frequency financial data.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending neural-parameterized operator frameworks into continuous time, potentially using neural stochastic differential equations, to accommodate irregular observation intervals and natural market microstructure dynamics.

**Why It Matters:** Continuous-time formulations offer a more mathematically rigorous way to handle high-frequency, asynchronous financial data compared to arbitrary time-grid discretization.
