title: Bridging Predictive and Structural Causality
---

**Background:** Neural autoregressive models are frequently used for causal discovery, but their outputs often conflate predictive contribution magnitude with causal indispensability.

**Question / Future Work:** A central challenge remains in bridging the gap between predictive performance and structural causal interpretation, specifically by connecting forecast necessity to sensitivity analysis or partial identification to better distinguish between purely predictive and structural causal effects.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for moving beyond purely behavioral metrics toward formalizing the link between machine learning model predictions and true structural causal relationships.

**Evidence:** Connecting forecast necessity to sensitivity analysis or partial identification could help bridge the gap between predictive and structural causal reasoning.
