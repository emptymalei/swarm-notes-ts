title: Framework Validation Against Data
---

**Background:** Vehicle energy simulation tools often lack established procedures for reconciling simulated subsystem outputs with physical measurement data.

**Question / Future Work:** A methodology is required to systematically validate low-order physical models within modular simulation frameworks against empirical vehicle performance data to improve predictive accuracy and reliability.

**Why It Matters:** Validation is essential to transition simulation frameworks from comparative research tools to reliable predictive engineering instruments.

**Evidence:** The next step is straightforward: compare VEHRON against measured vehicle data.
