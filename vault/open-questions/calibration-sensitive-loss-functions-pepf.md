title: Calibration-Sensitive PEPF Loss Functions
---

**Background:** Probabilistic forecasts are evaluated using scoring rules like the Continuous Ranked Probability Score (CRPS) and Energy Score, but these metrics do not always prioritize the specific calibration or tail-risk characteristics required for operational decision-making.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to develop loss functions explicitly designed for well-calibrated probabilistic electricity price forecasts, particularly those that align with downstream decision-aware performance rather than relying solely on generic scoring rules.

**Why It Matters:** Inaccurate tail-risk estimation in energy markets can lead to significant financial loss, making the development of calibration-sensitive loss functions a priority for operational machine learning.
