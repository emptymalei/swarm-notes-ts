title: SIGW Reheating Transition Kernel
---

**Background:** Scalar-induced gravitational waves (SIGWs) are sensitive to the equation of state of the universe during the reheating epoch, which remains largely unexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of a precise analytic kernel for the SIGW spectrum that accounts for non-instantaneous transitions from the reheating phase to radiation domination is required to accurately model signatures entering sensitive interferometer bands.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical limitation for accurately forecasting signal detectability and performing parameter estimation for gravitational wave observatories like LISA when the reheating scale is within the sensitive range.

**Evidence:** the precise feature depends on the transition to radiation domination and an exact analytical kernel is currently unavailable.
