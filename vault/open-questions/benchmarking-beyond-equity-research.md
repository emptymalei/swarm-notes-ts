title: Benchmarking Beyond Equity Research
---

**Background:** Financial investment research tasks, such as macro-economic, commodity, and credit research, demand specialized analytical skills and domain knowledge distinct from those required for equity research. Existing benchmarks primarily focus on equity research, leaving the capabilities of deep research agents in these other essential financial domains largely unexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to extend benchmarking frameworks beyond equity research to include other asset classes like credit, commodities, and macro-economics. These domains may require different qualitative and quantitative analytical architectures and specialized metrics tailored to their unique market characteristics.

**Why It Matters:** Expanding the scope of AI evaluation to broader financial domains is critical for developing comprehensive, domain-specialized research agents that can support the full spectrum of institutional investment needs.
