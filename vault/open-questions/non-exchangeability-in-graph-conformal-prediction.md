title: Non-exchangeability in graph conformal prediction
---

**Background:** Conformal prediction requires the exchangeability of data samples to guarantee finite-sample coverage, but this assumption is frequently violated in graph-structured time series due to temporal dependencies and cross-node couplings.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing more generalizable coverage guarantees for conformal prediction in complex, non-stationary, or non-linearly coupled spatio-temporal systems remains a significant open challenge. Future research is needed to determine the limits of spectral conditioning and to formalize exchangeability for dynamic topologies.

**Why It Matters:** This question addresses the fundamental tension between strict coverage guarantees and the pervasive non-exchangeability found in real-world dynamical systems.
