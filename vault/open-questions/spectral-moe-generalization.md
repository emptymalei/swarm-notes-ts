title: MoE Architecture Adaptation
---

**Background:** Spectral diagnostics for large language model optimization have been demonstrated on decoder-only architectures. Adapting these metrics to more complex architectures is a required step for broader application.

**Question / Future Work:** The current taxonomic framework for architectural acceleration was established on specific decoder-only cumulative intervention families. It is not known how these spectral diagnostic signals manifest or require adaptation in Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures or other non-standard Transformer variants.

**Why It Matters:** MoE models have different structural characteristics and training dynamics, making it necessary to understand whether existing spectral diagnostic signatures generalize or if they require new calibration for these widespread model architectures.
