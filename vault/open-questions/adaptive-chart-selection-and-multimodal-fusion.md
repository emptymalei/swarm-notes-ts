title: Adaptive Chart Selection and Multimodal Fusion Strategies
---

**Background:** Time-series classification (TSC) methods have increasingly utilized visual chart representations, yet there is no unified understanding of how different chart types and fusion strategies interact with raw sequence data across diverse domains.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is needed to develop adaptive chart generation techniques that dynamically select the most informative visual representation based on signal characteristics. This includes automating modality selection or importance weighting to prevent the introduction of redundant or noisy features in multimodal fusion pipelines.

**Why It Matters:** Current multimodal fusion approaches often rely on fixed strategies that may not account for task-specific noise or redundancy, leading to inconsistent performance. Developing adaptive, data-driven frameworks for chart selection and fusion is essential to achieve robust and scalable multimodal time-series classification.
