title: Multivariate time series GP extension
---

**Background:** Time series classification often involves multivariate data where inter-variable correlations provide significant discriminative information. Current feature-based evolutionary approaches, however, are primarily restricted to univariate series.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending genetic programming frameworks for time series classification to handle multivariate datasets requires developing novel program structures and operators capable of capturing and utilizing cross-variate dependencies without introducing excessive computational complexity.

**Why It Matters:** Multivariate data is ubiquitous, and existing evolutionary search techniques often struggle to scale to cross-variable dependencies effectively.

**Evidence:** Extending EvoTSC to handle multivariate time series, where cross-variable dependencies carry additional information, is a natural and important direction.
