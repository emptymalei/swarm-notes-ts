title: Heavy-tailed HMMs for Operational Risk
---

**Background:** Hidden Markov Models (HMMs) typically assume Gaussian emission distributions for analytical convenience when modeling operational risk losses. These distributions often fail to capture the heavy-tailed nature of actual loss data, which is characteristic of extreme events in financial risk management.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending multivariate HMM frameworks to incorporate heavy-tailed elliptical distributions, such as the Student-t distribution, to better account for extreme risk events.

**Why It Matters:** Standard HMM formulations frequently underestimate the frequency and magnitude of extreme risks, which is a critical limitation for applications in finance and operational risk.
