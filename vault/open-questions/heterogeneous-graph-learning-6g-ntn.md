title: Heterogeneous Graph Learning for 6G
---

**Background:** Integrating low Earth orbit (LEO) satellite networks with terrestrial infrastructure and other non-terrestrial networks requires a unified management framework capable of representing diverse network elements.

**Question / Future Work:** Existing graph learning frameworks for LEO constellations need to be extended into heterogeneous graphs (HeteroGNNs) that can account for different node capabilities and connection types across terrestrial, aerial (UAVs/HAPs), and space-borne network segments. Research must address how to learn a unified latent space that allows seamless orchestration across these inherently different infrastructure layers.

**Why It Matters:** Heterogeneity is an essential characteristic of 6G non-terrestrial networks, and current homogeneous GNN models cannot effectively optimize resources across multi-domain environments.
