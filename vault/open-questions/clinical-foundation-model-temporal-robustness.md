title: Foundation Model Temporal Robustness
---

**Background:** Clinical foundation models process highly heterogeneous longitudinal data spanning decades, but their representations are often siloed or lack global semantic alignment across multimodal inputs.

**Question / Future Work:** Understanding the limits of generalized clinical forecasting, particularly in ensuring that virtual patient representations remain robust to changes in clinical practice, diagnostic technology, and hospital workflows over decades.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical bottleneck for deploying foundation models in clinical settings where data distribution shifts significantly over time.
