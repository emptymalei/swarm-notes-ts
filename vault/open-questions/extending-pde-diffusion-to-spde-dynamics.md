title: Extending PDE-Diffusion to SPDEs
---

**Background:** Spatiotemporal forecasting models frequently struggle with the trade-off between capturing complex dynamics and maintaining long-term physical consistency, often leading to error accumulation.

**Question / Future Work:** Generalizing PDE-regularized diffusion models to stochastic partial differential equation (SPDE) regimes remains a critical open challenge for modeling inherently stochastic dynamical systems, particularly when moving beyond deterministic PDE-governed evolution.

**Why It Matters:** Many real-world dynamical systems are inherently stochastic; bridging the gap between deterministic PDE-constrained models and stochastic spatiotemporal evolution is essential for robust forecasting.

**Evidence:** we plan to extend this framework to SPDE-governed regimes in which the underlying evolution is intrinsically stochastic.
