title: CLV Theory High-Dimensional Extension
---

**Background:** Dynamical systems theory provides a rigorous framework for identifying precursors to critical transitions, yet generalizing these results to high-dimensional systems is a significant challenge due to computational complexity and the nature of spectral gaps.

**Question / Future Work:** The transition from low-dimensional model validation to high-dimensional multiscale systems remains a primary bottleneck for deploying physically-informed extreme event forecasting in real-world environments like climate systems or infrastructure.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between theory and high-dimensional applicability is essential for making this methodology practical for real-world catastrophe prevention.

**Evidence:** Future work will focus on extending the framework to higher-dimensional multiscale systems.
