title: Validator-Loss Metagraph Measurement
---

**Background:** The validator-loss function is a proposed mechanism for scoring predictive agents within a decentralized Bittensor subnet. It has been specified formally but remains unimplemented against a live multi-miner metagraph.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to implement and measure the proposed three-component validator loss—which combines Brier score, inconsistency metrics, and calibration error—against an active, multi-miner network to determine its effectiveness and robustness in practice.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental step in testing the mechanism's ability to resist gaming and reward genuinely accurate predictive agents in a decentralized system.

**Evidence:** The specification is a design proposal: this paper does not yet measure the loss against a live multi-miner metagraph.
