title: Supernova Progenitor Mass Weighting
---

**Background:** The diffuse supernova axion-induced gamma-ray flux depends on the assumed distribution of supernova progenitor masses, but current models often rely on a limited set of individual progenitor profiles.

**Question / Future Work:** Incorporating a realistic, statistically weighted distribution of progenitor mass profiles into the axion emission framework is necessary to improve the robustness of diffuse gamma-ray flux predictions.

**Why It Matters:** The use of limited benchmark profiles introduces significant model-dependent uncertainty that hampers the reliability of axion-photon coupling constraints.
