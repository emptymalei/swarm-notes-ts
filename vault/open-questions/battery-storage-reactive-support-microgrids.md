title: Battery storage for reactive support
---

**Background:** High-PV islanded microgrids often encounter feasibility limits due to reactive-voltage constraints that cannot be fully captured by traditional real-power dispatch. Integrating battery storage as a flexible, multi-functional reactive power resource remains a key challenge for infrastructure planning and operational optimization.

**Question / Future Work:** The integration of battery energy storage systems (BESS) as an alternative or complement to passive capacitor placement requires evaluating their dual-role in managing both energy time-shifting and instantaneous reactive power support to maintain system feasibility under variable PV penetration.

**Why It Matters:** Expanding reactive support infrastructure beyond passive capacitors to include active storage systems is essential for improving the hosting capacity and operational flexibility of islanded microgrids.
