title: Scalability of Multifunctional Reservoir Computing
---

**Background:** Reservoir computing models for inferring chaotic dynamics are typically designed for single systems and may face performance degradation when extended to infer multiple systems simultaneously.

**Question / Future Work:** As the number of target systems increases in a multifunctional reservoir computing (MFRC) scheme, the impact on the accuracy and reliability of the replicated attractors and the quality of the reconstructed bifurcation diagrams remains to be quantified.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the scalability limit of MFRC schemes is essential for their deployment in complex scenarios involving more than two chaotic systems.

**Evidence:** Although it is generally expected that the machine performance will degrade as the number of target systems increases, a detailed analysis is still needed to quantify the impact of this increase on the accuracy and reliability of the replicated attractors, as well as on the quality of the reconstructed bifurcation diagrams.
