title: Market-Model Hybrid Forecasting Systems
---

**Background:** Prediction markets serve as effective information aggregation mechanisms that often provide robust signals, particularly near the resolution of market events.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should explore hybrid forecasting systems that intelligently integrate agentic model predictions with real-time market prices, using conditional weighting policies informed by temporal proximity to resolution and market-specific uncertainty levels.

**Why It Matters:** Integrating AI reasoning with established information aggregation mechanisms is a key frontier for creating reliable, decision-support systems.
