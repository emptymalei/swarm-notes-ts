title: Multivariate quantum data processing
---

**Background:** Quantum reservoir computing has traditionally been applied to classical data streams, but emerging research is exploring its potential for processing natively quantum data. Effectively managing multidimensional quantum information within these systems is an area requiring further investigation.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to determine how to effectively process multivariate quantum data, such as dynamically evolving states of multipartite quantum systems, within a quantum reservoir computing framework. This includes defining appropriate encoding, measurement, and processing protocols for complex quantum inputs.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for the evolution of quantum reservoir computing into a natively quantum machine learning paradigm capable of processing quantum-coherent information.

**Evidence:** An open and intriguing question is how to effectively process multivariate quantum data, such as dynamically evolving states of multipartite quantum systems.
