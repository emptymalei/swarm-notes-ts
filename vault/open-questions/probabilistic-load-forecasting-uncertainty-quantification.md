title: Transitioning to Probabilistic Forecasting
---

**Background:** Most load forecasting models provide point estimates, limiting their utility in risk-aware decision-making scenarios where the full predictive distribution is required. Transitioning these models to capture predictive uncertainty is a core research challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work aims to evolve existing deterministic building energy forecasting architectures into probabilistic frameworks to explicitly quantify predictive uncertainty.

**Why It Matters:** Quantifying uncertainty is essential for risk-aware decision-making in demand response and energy management systems.
