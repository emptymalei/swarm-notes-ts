title: Advanced Computational Paradigms for Pricing
---

**Background:** Financial option pricing models often struggle to incorporate complex, high-frequency market dynamics, leading to discrepancies between theoretical estimates and observed market prices.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research explores integrating neural networks to optimize stochastic model parameters and investigating quantum walks for simulating price paths to address precision limitations in derivative modeling.

**Why It Matters:** The paper explicitly identifies these as necessary next-generation approaches to overcome the limitations of classical stochastic pricing models like Heston and Merton.
