title: Mechanisms of Skewness Dispersion
---

**Background:** Skewness dispersion serves as a significant predictor of aggregate market returns, with its predictive strength varying alongside macroeconomic policy announcements.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to theoretically decouple the relative contributions of rational risk compensation versus behavioral belief heterogeneity in the predictive signal of skewness dispersion, particularly by formalizing the interaction between higher-order moment dispersion and macroeconomic information flow.

**Why It Matters:** Clarifying the underlying economic drivers is essential for determining if this signal is a structural feature of market risk or a transient artifact of behavioral biases.

**Evidence:** We conclude that the observed return predictability reflects both the repricing of aggregate risk ... and the adjustment of valuation discrepancies ... in response to major macroeconomic announcements.
