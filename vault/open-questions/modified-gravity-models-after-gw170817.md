title: Investigate post-GW170817 gravity models
---

**Background:** Future testing of modified gravity models requires new, viable theoretical frameworks that can be constrained by future cluster surveys.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should investigate how modified gravity models that remain viable after the constraints from GW170817 (which constrained the speed of gravitational waves) alter the gravitational potentials of galaxy clusters.

**Why It Matters:** This is crucial for connecting observational constraints from gravitational redshifts to the next generation of viable modified gravity theories beyond General Relativity.

**Evidence:** Future work should be devoted to investigating how more modified gravity models surviving the GW170817 detection alter the gravitational potentials of clusters (Ezquiaga and Zumalacárregui, 2017).
