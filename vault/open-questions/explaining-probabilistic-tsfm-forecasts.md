title: Explaining Probabilistic TSFM Forecasts
---

**Background:** Time series foundation models are increasingly used for probabilistic forecasting, which provides critical uncertainty estimates for risk-sensitive decision-making.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending post-hoc explainability frameworks from point forecasts to explain probabilistic outputs and uncertainty quantification remains an open challenge, particularly for regulatory and safety-critical applications.

**Why It Matters:** Transparency in uncertainty is a fundamental requirement for the integration of AI models into grid operations and other risk-sensitive systems.

**Evidence:** Extending our framework to explain probabilistic forecasts and uncertainty quantification would address a critical gap for risk-sensitive operational decisions.
