title: Extending Supply Chain Digital Twin
---

**Background:** Supply chain networks often lack robust, interpretable simulators that link demand series with underlying logistics topology, capacity constraints, and control policies.

**Question / Future Work:** Identifying ways to extend supply chain digital twins to incorporate sophisticated multi-region shock models, external covariates, and advanced control policies is crucial for improving the realism of forecasting benchmarks.

**Why It Matters:** This is a substantial bottleneck for evaluating the robustness of forecasting models against complex logistical phenomena like cascading bottlenecks and regime shifts.
