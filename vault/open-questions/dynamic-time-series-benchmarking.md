title: Dynamic Time-Series Benchmarking
---

**Background:** Time-series forecasting evaluation frameworks frequently rely on static benchmark datasets that are often included in the pre-training corpora of foundation models, leading to data leakage and inflated performance estimates.

**Question / Future Work:** The research community needs dynamic forecasting benchmarks where test data is continuously refreshed or rotated to mitigate overfitting and ensure realistic assessments of model generalization.

**Why It Matters:** As foundation models dominate the research space, the lack of robust, non-contaminated evaluation benchmarks threatens the validity of reported model advancements.
