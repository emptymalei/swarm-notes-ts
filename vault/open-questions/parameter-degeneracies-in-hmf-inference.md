title: HMF Parameter Degeneracy Resolution
---

**Background:** Strong lensing analysis involves marginalizing over individual lens and subhalo parameters to constrain population-level properties, which frequently leads to parameter degeneracies.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating and breaking the inherent degeneracies in gravitational lensing, particularly between different mass function slopes and amplitudes, is necessary to improve the precision of inferred dark matter properties.

**Why It Matters:** Parameter degeneracy is a fundamental limitation in astronomical inference that directly reduces the constraining power of large-scale surveys.
