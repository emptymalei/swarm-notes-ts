title: Multivariate and Multi-step SCDR Extension
---

**Background:** Sequential Conformalized Density Regions (SCDR) is a method for generating time-series prediction sets with guaranteed asymptotic conditional coverage. Current research in this domain focuses on univariate time-series, leaving extensions to multivariate data sets and multi-step prediction horizons as open challenges.

**Question / Future Work:** The current framework for SCDR is primarily designed for univariate time-series prediction. Extending the method to handle multivariate responses and joint H-step ahead trajectory coverage, while maintaining computational feasibility and valid coverage guarantees, remains a non-trivial research challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Reliable multi-step and multivariate uncertainty quantification is critical for complex decision-making and control tasks in time-series analysis where cross-variable dependencies must be modeled.
