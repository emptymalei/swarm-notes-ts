title: Cross-dataset GP knowledge transfer
---

**Background:** Genetic programming applications often evolve models from scratch for each dataset, which is computationally expensive and redundant. Knowledge transfer could potentially accelerate this process by sharing evolved building blocks.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating methods to transfer or reuse evolved model components, such as effective subtrees or optimized operator sequences, across related time series classification tasks remains an open challenge for scaling GP-based AutoML.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for scaling evolutionary learning methods and reducing redundant computation in resource-constrained environments.

**Evidence:** Investigating how to transfer or reuse building blocks, such as effective subtree structures, across related datasets could potentially accelerate the evolutionary search.
