title: Robust Mobile MC Detection Bottlenecks
---

**Background:** Molecular communication (MC) systems often operate in environments where channel state information is limited and transmitter-receiver geometry is subject to unknown fluctuations. Such uncertainty can render traditional mean-level detection unreliable, necessitating the development of robust detection strategies that can exploit higher-order statistical properties.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating the theoretical and practical performance limits of joint mean–dispersion detection fusion in molecular communication, particularly in the low-count regime or under significant model mismatch.

**Why It Matters:** This is a foundational performance bottleneck in the transition from theoretical model-based detection to robust, adaptive nanonetwork transceivers.
