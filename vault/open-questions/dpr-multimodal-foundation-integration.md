title: Integration of Multimodal Covariates
---

**Background:** Real-world time series data frequently exhibit non-stationary, volatile dynamics, which limit the effectiveness of forecasting models that rely on globally shared, static weight transformations.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating the integration of multimodal covariates and DPR-like dynamic modulation within large-scale time series foundation models remains an open challenge for improved anticipatory feature recalibration.

**Why It Matters:** This explores the scalability and robustness of adaptive modulation mechanisms when applied to foundation model paradigms versus standard backbones.

**Evidence:** Future work will integrate multimodal covariates and embed DPR within large-scale pretrained models for anticipatory recalibration.
