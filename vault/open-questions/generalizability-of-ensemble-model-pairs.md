title: Generalizability of ensemble model-pairs
---

**Background:** Short-term load forecasting for computing-driven workloads is challenged by bursty, non-stationary patterns that deviate from traditional grid loads.

**Question / Future Work:** While ensemble methods can improve accuracy, it remains unclear whether specific submodel complementarities are universal or highly dependent on local job scheduling and data center architecture. Future work is required to determine the generalizability of these hybrid model pairs across heterogeneous infrastructure configurations.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the robustness of ensemble structures beyond individual case studies is essential for reliable deployment in grid-interactive applications.
