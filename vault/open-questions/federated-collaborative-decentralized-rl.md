title: Federated decentralized learning mechanisms
---

**Background:** Federated or collaborative learning mechanisms enable distributed reinforcement learning agents to share knowledge without compromising system scalability or individual agent privacy.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing federated or collaborative learning frameworks is required to allow decentralized agents to share their learned policies or experience buffers, thereby accelerating convergence and improving global system performance while maintaining decentralized operations and privacy-preserving data handling.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the trade-off between decentralized training efficiency and the potential for collective intelligence in distributed reinforcement learning architectures.
