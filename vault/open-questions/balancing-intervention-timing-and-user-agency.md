title: Intervention timing vs agency
---

**Background:** Proactive AI interventions aim to anticipate collaborative breakdowns to minimize intrusive feedback, but current models often struggle to balance the timing of these interventions with the preservation of human user agency.

**Question / Future Work:** Determine the optimal threshold for intervening in collaborative tasks that balances the objective improvement of team performance against the subjective preservation of individual user agency and autonomy, particularly when moving from reactive threshold-based triggers to predictive forecasting models.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical bottleneck in the design of collaborative AI agents that aim to act as seamless teammates rather than intrusive monitors.

**Evidence:** Proactive feedback better preserves learner agency by maintaining optimal collaboration states, particularly for high-performing pairs.
