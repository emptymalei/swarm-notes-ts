title: Scalable distributed GP inference
---

**Background:** Distributed estimation systems require robust and scalable inference methods to manage data across multiple agents under communication and resource constraints.

**Question / Future Work:** Scalability in multi-agent networks, where a large number of sensors or robots must coordinate their inference without a central controller, remains an open research problem. Future work is needed to explore consensus-based Gaussian process filtering and distributed variational inference frameworks that can operate effectively under strict communication bandwidth and computational limits.

**Why It Matters:** As signal processing systems become increasingly decentralized, developing scalable, fault-tolerant inference mechanisms for distributed Gaussian processes is essential for practical deployment.
