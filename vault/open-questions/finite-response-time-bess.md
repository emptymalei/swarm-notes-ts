title: BESS Finite Response Time
---

**Background:** The methodology assumes that battery energy storage systems (BESS) controllers respond instantaneously to fluctuations in primary power source output.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the implications of a finite response time of the BESS controller on the accuracy and stability of the derived stationary probability density function for reflected random walks used in ramp-rate control.

**Why It Matters:** Real-world BESS systems have non-zero ramp rates and communication/processing delays that could significantly impact the effectiveness of the control strategy and the validity of the stationary PDF derived under the assumption of instantaneous response.

**Evidence:** The effect of a finite response time of the BESS controller will be assessed in future work.
