title: Multi-step Traffic Prediction Horizons
---

**Background:** Many operational Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) applications require multi-step ahead forecasts to facilitate proactive signal control.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending hierarchical flow decomposition to support multi-step prediction horizons involves addressing the challenges of error accumulation and temporal dependency modeling over longer prediction windows.

**Why It Matters:** Multi-step prediction is a critical requirement for proactive adaptive signal control, yet many hierarchical models are optimized for short-term, single-interval predictions.

**Evidence:** Future work will extend the framework to corridors with diverse geometric and demand conditions, multi-step prediction horizons, and integration with signal timing optimization.
