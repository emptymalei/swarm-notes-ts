title: Stochastic Model Parameter Optimization
---

**Background:** Financial option pricing models often struggle to incorporate complex, non-linear market dynamics, such as sudden price jumps, stochastic volatility, and liquidity constraints, into a unified computational framework.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to further investigate the integration of advanced neural networks for dynamic, real-time parameter optimization in stochastic models to better capture subtle, time-varying market patterns, alongside exploring alternative computational frameworks like quantum walks for simulating asset price paths.

**Why It Matters:** Developing more robust, adaptive, and computationally efficient simulation methods is crucial for precise derivative pricing and risk management in volatile environments where historical-only calibration often fails.
