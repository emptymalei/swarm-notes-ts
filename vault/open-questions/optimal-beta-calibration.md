title: Systematic beta calibration for SBBTS
---

**Background:** The regularization parameter in the Schrödinger-Bass Bridge framework controls the interpolation between drift-based and volatility-based dynamics, yet systematic, automated methods for its optimal selection are underdeveloped.

**Question / Future Work:** The performance of the SBBTS model is sensitive to the choice of the regularization parameter, which currently lacks a principled or systematic calibration criterion. Developing an automated approach to determine this parameter would improve the model's adaptability across different financial datasets and market regimes.

**Why It Matters:** A systematic way to tune this hyperparameter is critical for practitioners to ensure consistent and optimal performance without relying on manual trial-and-error or heuristics across diverse financial contexts.
