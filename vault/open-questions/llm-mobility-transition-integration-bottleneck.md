title: LLM Mobility Transition Integration
---

**Background:** Human mobility prediction models often struggle with transitions that appear rarely or never in training data, which constitutes a core performance bottleneck. Current generative recommendation models exhibit varying capabilities in generalizing across these unseen or sparse transition patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the integration of domain-specific transition-level mobility models with general-purpose generative AI, specifically focusing on grounding world knowledge within mobility transitions to improve long-tail accuracy and interpretability.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the identified bottleneck of transition-level sparsity while bridging the gap between traditional mobility modeling and emerging generative AI approaches.
