title: Spatio-temporal rainfall propagation modeling
---

**Background:** Rainfall patterns are influenced by complex, multi-scale interactions between atmospheric conditions and topography, making the prediction of rainfall behavior under changing environmental climates a significant challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to extend site-specific stochastic jump-diffusion rainfall models to incorporate spatio-temporal correlations for investigating how extreme events propagate across geographical regions.

**Why It Matters:** Transitioning from site-specific modeling to regional or global climate scales is critical for accurately simulating the propagation of extreme weather events.

**Evidence:** One natural extension is to incorporate spatio-temporal correlations to study how extreme rainfall events propagate across regions
