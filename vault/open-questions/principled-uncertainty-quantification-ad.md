title: Principled Uncertainty Quantification for AD
---

**Background:** Deep learning-based anomaly detectors typically produce point estimates that do not naturally convey the reliability of an alarm, leading to high rates of false positives in critical industrial infrastructure.

**Question / Future Work:** Implementing more principled uncertainty quantification techniques is necessary to distinguish high-confidence anomalies from low-confidence signals, which could significantly reduce the burden of inspecting false positives.

**Why It Matters:** Operators require reliable signals to manage large-scale systems effectively; uncertainty quantification directly addresses the critical bottleneck of actionable alarm generation.
