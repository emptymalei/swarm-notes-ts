title: Multimodal Urban Context Integration
---

**Background:** Foundation models for spatio-temporal data primarily process numerical sequences but often struggle to incorporate exogenous multimodal urban contextual data such as event logs, infrastructure layouts, or dynamic policies.

**Question / Future Work:** How can unified spatio-temporal foundation models effectively integrate exogenous multimodal urban contexts to improve predictive robustness and causal interpretability in real-world scenarios?

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond purely signal-based sequence modeling to incorporate heterogeneous urban context is essential for building comprehensive 'urban foundation models'.
