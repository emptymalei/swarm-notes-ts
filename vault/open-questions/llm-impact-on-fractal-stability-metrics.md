title: LLM Impact on Fractal Metrics
---

**Background:** Software development processes are increasingly influenced by automated and generative AI agents, which may fundamentally alter the temporal structure of code contribution streams.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research must determine whether fractal scaling indicators of process stability (like alpha) remain valid when commit generation is driven or heavily augmented by LLMs rather than purely human creative processes. This requires evaluating whether these agents introduce different correlation structures or if the existing stability-correlation link persists across heterogeneous development modalities.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for evaluating whether established human-centric process metrics maintain their validity in the era of AI-augmented software engineering.
