title: Cross-Platform Forecasting Integration
---

**Background:** Prediction markets serve as decentralized mechanisms for aggregating information by trading claims on future events. While individual platforms provide data, the lack of standardized, cross-platform schemas inhibits large-scale analysis of collective belief discovery and macroeconomic forecasting.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to develop standardized, cross-platform protocols for entity resolution, market definitions, and oracle event schemas to enable comprehensive, multi-platform longitudinal studies of information aggregation and price discovery within the decentralized forecasting ecosystem.

**Why It Matters:** Generalizing across platforms is essential to decouple platform-specific artifacts from underlying forecasting signals and to evaluate market efficiency at scale.

**Evidence:** the data lifecycle of a prediction market remains highly fragmented... there is still no unified and continuously maintained dataset that captures the full lifecycle of decentralized prediction markets
