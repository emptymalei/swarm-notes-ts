title: PINN Scalability in Finance
---

**Background:** Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) integrate governing equations into the neural network training process as structural constraints. They are increasingly being explored for financial applications where data-driven predictions must adhere to established theoretical financial models.

**Question / Future Work:** The scalability and effectiveness of PINNs in complex, high-dimensional portfolio management scenarios remains an unresolved research challenge. There is a need for robust, standardized validation protocols across diverse, noisy, and high-dimensional financial datasets to benchmark their performance against established numerical and machine learning methods.

**Why It Matters:** The technical viability of PINNs in finance depends on their ability to generalize beyond stylized toy models to real-world, high-dimensional financial environments.
