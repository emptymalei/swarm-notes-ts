title: Multi-Agent SBFD Resource Allocation
---

**Background:** Resource allocation in Sub-Band Full Duplex (SBFD) systems must account for interference management, traffic burstiness, and the trade-offs between uplink and downlink performance. Scaling these approaches to multi-cell environments and validating them against real-world traffic data remains an open challenge for 6G deployment.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to extend single-agent reinforcement learning frameworks for SBFD resource allocation to multi-agent, multi-cell scenarios while simultaneously evaluating these models against empirical network traffic datasets rather than synthetic traffic models.

**Why It Matters:** Multi-agent coordination is required for inter-cell interference management in dense 6G deployments, and empirical validation is essential for proving real-world applicability beyond simulated Markov-modulated processes.
