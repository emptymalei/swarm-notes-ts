title: Unified Forecasting Augmentation Benchmarking
---

**Background:** Data augmentation techniques in time series forecasting are often evaluated under non-uniform experimental protocols, complicating the isolation of performance gains.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an unresolved need for comprehensive, standardized benchmarking protocols to evaluate the efficacy of various time series augmentation methods across diverse architectures and datasets, ensuring fair comparisons and robust validation.

**Why It Matters:** Without standardized benchmarks, assessing the generalizability and true impact of novel augmentation strategies like TPS remains problematic for the field.

**Evidence:** Decomposition-based and other augmentation techniques (Bandara et al., 2021; Semenoglou et al., 2023; Zhang et al., 2023) have also shown promise, though comprehensive comparisons under unified fair experimental settings remain limited.
