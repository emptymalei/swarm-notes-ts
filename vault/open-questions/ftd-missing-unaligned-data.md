title: FTD adaptation for missing data
---

**Background:** The current FTD framework is designed for data where the continuous mode is sampled coherently, but real-world data can feature missing values or unaligned sampling schemes.

**Question / Future Work:** Develop adaptations of the functional Tucker decomposition that explicitly address missing data or unaligned sampling schemes in the functional mode, building upon existing work in related areas.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing missing or unaligned functional data is a common practical challenge, and integrating solutions directly into the FTD framework would substantially increase its utility in real-world scenarios.

**Evidence:** Further extensions of the present method include adaptations that address missing data or unaligned sampling schemes, in line with [8, 15].
