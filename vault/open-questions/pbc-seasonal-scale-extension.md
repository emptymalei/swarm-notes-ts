title: PBC for seasonal forecasting
---

**Background:** Subseasonal weather forecasting is constrained by rapidly accumulating modeling errors and atmospheric interdependencies, often resulting in degradation at long lead times. While probabilistic bias correction (PBC) targets systematic errors within these windows, its capacity to mitigate biases at longer, seasonal scales remains untested.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the feasibility and performance of the PBC framework when extended to seasonal-scale forecasting. Research should determine if learned corrections generalize to these longer horizons or if the framework requires architectural changes to address distinct physical drivers of seasonal predictability.

**Why It Matters:** Extending bias correction beyond subseasonal scales could improve long-term planning for agriculture and energy, which currently depend on forecasts with limited skill.
