title: Unstructured Knowledge Integration for Forecasting
---

**Background:** Multimodal time series forecasting often employs LLMs to provide semantic priors, typically using deep synchronous fusion strategies where interactions occur at every network layer, leading to potential semantic interference.

**Question / Future Work:** The transition from synthetic, rule-based prompts to rich, unstructured external knowledge is essential for improving the real-world utility and reasoning capability of LLM-based time series models. Determining how to effectively align such high-dimensional, unstructured knowledge with the hierarchical asynchronous fusion framework remains an unresolved challenge.

**Why It Matters:** The transition from synthetic, rule-based prompts to rich, unstructured external knowledge is essential for improving the real-world utility and reasoning capability of LLM-based time series models.
