title: Structured Knowledge Accumulation for ML Agents
---

**Background:** Autonomous AI development agents rely on parametric knowledge within LLMs and general-purpose tools to guide decision-making. Systematic approaches for integrating domain-specific scientific knowledge into these agents are currently underdeveloped.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to design structured, retrievable knowledge bases containing modeling strategies, architecture patterns, and empirical outcomes that can be dynamically updated, allowing autonomous systems to accumulate expertise and improve over time.

**Why It Matters:** Structured knowledge accumulation is a critical bottleneck for evolving these agents from zero-shot reasoning to cumulative, expert-level system performance.

**Evidence:** Incorporating a structured AI task knowledge base... could improve both the quality and consistency of the system’s decisions. ... Such a knowledge base could be... dynamically updated after each run with newly discovered strategies.
