title: Recall Evaluation for Trends
---

**Background:** Topical trend detection in social media platforms requires the evaluation of performance metrics beyond simple precision. Measuring the recall of emerging trends is challenging in large-scale environments due to the absence of a comprehensive ground-truth dataset.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing reliable methods to measure recall in production-scale trend detection systems remains a significant open challenge. Without a definitive ground-truth or benchmark, it is difficult to determine how many emerging trends are missed by the system, hindering comprehensive performance evaluation.

**Why It Matters:** Precision-only evaluation is insufficient for understanding system coverage, making the development of automated ground-truth generation or recall estimation techniques essential for improving the discovery of novel and emerging trends.

**Evidence:** We do not report recall due to the absence of a comprehensive global ground-truth dataset.
