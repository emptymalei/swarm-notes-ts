title: Automated physical group discovery
---

**Background:** Wind power forecasting relies on meteorological variables to predict generation, yet the optimal selection of these variables and their integration remain highly site- and condition-dependent. Current deep learning approaches often employ uniform mixing or dimensionality reduction without exploiting the physical structure of meteorological data.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of automated, data-driven group discovery mechanisms for exogenous variable selection remains an open challenge. Current methods rely on manual, domain-informed grouping priors, and automating this process could enhance model applicability in scenarios where physical relationships are complex or non-obvious.

**Why It Matters:** Developing data-driven group discovery would reduce the reliance on expert domain knowledge and improve the portability of forecasting frameworks to new sites where physical relationships are not well-characterized.
