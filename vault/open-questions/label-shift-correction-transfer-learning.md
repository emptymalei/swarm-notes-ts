title: Label Shift Correction Mechanisms
---

**Background:** Label shift involves discrepancies in the marginal distribution of target variables across domains, which frequently degrades the performance of transfer learning models.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to integrate explicit label-shift correction mechanisms into distribution-aware transfer learning architectures, as this remains a primary bottleneck for maintaining performance under cross-domain transfer in data-scarce environments. Developing techniques that can detect and compensate for label distribution shifts without relying on extensive labeled target data is critical for robust financial forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** Label shift is identified as the most significant hurdle for transfer learning effectiveness in financial forecasting scenarios, and current architectures often lack mechanisms to explicitly mitigate this type of distributional drift.
