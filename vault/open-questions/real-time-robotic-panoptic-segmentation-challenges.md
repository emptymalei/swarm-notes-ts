title: Real-time Robotic Panoptic Segmentation Challenges
---

**Background:** Panoptic segmentation performs simultaneous semantic and instance segmentation to provide comprehensive scene understanding, but its application to large-scale, sparse sensor data in unstructured environments is computationally demanding.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending current offline panoptic segmentation architectures to support single-scan inference and integrating them into onboard SLAM frameworks is necessary for enabling real-time semantic map generation on robotic platforms in unstructured environments.

**Why It Matters:** Real-time onboard semantic awareness is essential for advanced robotic behaviors such as selective navigation and precise environmental interaction, which cannot rely on post-mission data processing.
