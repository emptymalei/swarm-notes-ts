title: Adaptive Periodicity Learning
---

**Background:** Time series forecasting models often rely on predefined cycle lengths to handle periodic non-stationarity.

**Question / Future Work:** Current frameworks frequently assume fixed or manually specified cycle lengths for periodic modeling, limiting their adaptability to time series with varying, multi-scale, or non-dominant periodicities. Future research is required to develop mechanisms for automatically learning or dynamically adjusting periodicity parameters from data.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a significant hyperparameter bottleneck that limits the robustness and generalizability of cycle-aware forecasting models in scenarios where periodicity is not constant or known a priori.
