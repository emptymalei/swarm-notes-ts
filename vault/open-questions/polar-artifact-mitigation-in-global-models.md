title: Polar Artifact Mitigation in Global Models
---

**Background:** Learned atmospheric models often rely on Cartesian grids, which introduce topological artifacts near the Earth's poles when using standard convolutional kernels.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing efficient geometric inductive biases or grid treatments for 2D convolutional architectures that eliminate systematic polar artifacts in global forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** Polar artifacts represent a persistent systematic failure mode that limits the physical fidelity of global forecasting models.

**Evidence:** our power spectra and qualitative analyses reveal systematic artifacts... likely stemming from our minimalist treatment of the sphere.
