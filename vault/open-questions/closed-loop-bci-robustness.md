title: Closed-loop BCI Robustness
---

**Background:** Neural population models are often evaluated on their ability to perform next-step spike forecasting, yet the impact of different forecasting architectures and objectives on implicit behavioral decoding remains underexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate whether implicit behavioral decoding gains generalize across different experimental sites and whether such pipelines can achieve robust performance in fully closed-loop BCI implementations on live animal models.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the gap between predictive performance in static benchmarks and real-world utility in autonomous closed-loop brain-computer interfaces.
