title: Automated hyperparameter optimization
---

**Background:** Hyperparameter optimization in neuromorphic models, specifically those based on reservoir computing, is often performed manually due to the small number of trainable parameters and the lack of gradient-based learning.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing a systematic, automated hyperparameter optimization strategy to replace manual trial-and-error processes is necessary to improve predictive performance and simplify the model's deployment for reservoir computing architectures.

**Why It Matters:** The reliance on manual tuning acts as a bottleneck for scaling reservoir models to new domains, hindering their usability in broader applications.
