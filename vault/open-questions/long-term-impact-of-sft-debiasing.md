title: Long-term impact of SFT-debiasing
---

**Background:** Large language models often exhibit systematic extrapolation bias when forecasting, which is resistant to prompt-based interventions. While supervised fine-tuning can mitigate this bias in specific tasks, the durability and potential side effects of this intervention across broader contexts remain unclear.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to determine whether parameter-level interventions like supervised fine-tuning induce long-term changes in reasoning capabilities or if these adjustments lead to unforeseen side effects in other domains, including potential degradation in general capabilities.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the trade-offs between domain-specific bias correction and general model performance is essential for deploying reliable, autonomous AI agents in high-stakes environments.
