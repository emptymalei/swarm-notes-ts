title: Time-Series Conformal Coverage Guarantees
---

**Background:** Split-conformal prediction methods rely on data exchangeability, an assumption typically violated in time-series data.

**Question / Future Work:** There is no universal framework providing unconditional, finite-sample distribution-free coverage guarantees for conformal prediction in time-series forecasting settings. Further research is required to reconcile strong empirical performance with theoretical guarantees for dependent data.

**Why It Matters:** Theoretical validation is essential for establishing reliable prediction intervals in decision-critical forecasting applications.

**Evidence:** in the general time-series setting no conformal method delivers an unconditional finite-sample distribution-free coverage guarantee
