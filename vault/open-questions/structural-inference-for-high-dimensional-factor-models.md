title: Structural Inference for Factor Models
---

**Background:** Many high-dimensional time-series frameworks focus primarily on predictive accuracy rather than structural identification.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing generalized impulse response function (GIRF) estimation for dynamic factor SVM models to enable structural scenario analysis and causal transmission modeling.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between predictive models and structural inference is essential for policy-oriented applications of high-dimensional machine learning.

**Evidence:** While we have focused on forecasting, the proposed framework could be extended to structural analysis by computing generalized impulse response functions to trace the effects of uncertainty shocks.
