title: Recovering Core Methodological Dependencies
---

**Background:** Scientific discovery pathways represent the sequence of enabling contributions required to realize a target contribution. Current models struggle with high-granularity dependency reasoning, particularly for methodological mechanisms.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to address the persistent gap in identifying core methodological dependencies, which remain significantly harder to recover than other types of enabling contributions, as this is critical for generating actionable plans for new scientific methods.

**Why It Matters:** The difficulty in recovering core methodological dependencies is a critical bottleneck in automated scientific forecasting.

**Evidence:** Core methodological dependencies are the hardest enabling contributions to recover. ... This suggests that models can often name salient resources or pretrained backbones, but struggle to infer the specific methodological mechanisms needed to realize a target contribution.
