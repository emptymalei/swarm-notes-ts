title: Scaling Quantum-Inspired Module Overhead
---

**Background:** Hybrid quantum-classical machine learning architectures, particularly those for high-dimensional spatiotemporal forecasting, often rely on simulating quantum circuits on classical hardware which scales poorly with circuit complexity.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining efficient methods to minimize the computational overhead of quantum-inspired modules is essential for their practical scalability in operational, time-sensitive forecasting systems.

**Why It Matters:** This is a primary bottleneck for deploying quantum-inspired models in operational environments requiring high-throughput inference.
