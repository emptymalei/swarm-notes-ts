title: Multi-channel state discovery
---

**Background:** Complex systems are often composed of components with multiple coupled internal states, which complicates the modeling of their governing equations.

**Question / Future Work:** Future efforts should focus on extending current symbolic discovery frameworks to support multi-channel message passing and cross-variable basis functions to better capture the interactions between coupled internal state variables.

**Why It Matters:** Generalizing the framework to multi-dimensional internal states is critical for accurately modeling complex, high-dimensional dynamical systems like biological cells or advanced neural circuits.

**Evidence:** First, systems with coupled internal states may benefit from multi-channel message passing and cross-variable bases.
