title: Clinical Risk Calibration Reliability
---

**Background:** Large language models often struggle with faithful numerical computation and trend hallucination when interpreting longitudinal EHR data.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to move beyond static benchmark performance toward clinical risk calibration and robust evidence governance, specifically addressing how to translate reasoning artifacts into reliable, operationally safe clinical tools. Future research should prioritize prevalence-aware modeling and the identification of failure modes in heterogeneous real-world clinical deployment environments.

**Why It Matters:** Addresses the critical gap between model performance on benchmarks and the safety standards required for clinical decision support systems.
