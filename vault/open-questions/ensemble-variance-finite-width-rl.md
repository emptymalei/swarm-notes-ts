title: Ensemble variance in finite-width RL
---

**Background:** Deep learning ensembles in reinforcement learning settings often rely on experience replay buffers that contain temporally correlated data, challenging classical i.i.d. ensemble theory.

**Question / Future Work:** Formalizing the covariance limits and statistical guarantees for ensemble variance in finite-width neural networks trained with dynamically moving targets—without relying on Neural Tangent Kernel (NTK) simplifications—remains an unresolved problem.

**Why It Matters:** Critical for bridging the gap between theoretical models and practical deep RL implementations where non-linear dynamics and non-stationary targets are prevalent.
