title: Hybrid LLM-Temporal Architectures
---

**Background:** Large language models adapted for time-series analysis often rely on parameter-efficient techniques, but their performance in data-rich regimes frequently lags behind architectures explicitly designed for long-term temporal dependencies.

**Question / Future Work:** The challenge lies in designing hybrid model architectures that integrate explicit temporal inductive biases into large language model frameworks to bridge the performance gap between general-purpose foundation models and specialized time-series forecasting architectures.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical architectural trade-off that defines the utility of foundation models in specialized physical science domains.
