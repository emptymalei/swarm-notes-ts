title: RQA for non-ergodic systems
---

**Background:** Many-body localized systems and models with quantum many-body scars exhibit non-ergodic dynamics that break standard thermalization behavior.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains unclear whether recurrence quantification analysis (RQA) can effectively characterize the critical disorder strength for ergodicity breaking in many-body localized systems or identify periodic revivals in systems hosting quantum many-body scars. Establishing the limitations and diagnostic potential of RQA in these non-ergodic regimes is a significant open research direction.

**Why It Matters:** Extending RQA to non-ergodic quantum systems provides a unified, unsupervised framework for investigating complex dynamical phase transitions, where established metrics often fail.
