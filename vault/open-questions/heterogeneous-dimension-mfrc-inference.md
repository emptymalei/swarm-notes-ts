title: Multifunctional Inference of Heterogeneous Systems
---

**Background:** Reservoir computing schemes often assume that all target systems share the same state-space dimensionality for training and inference.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending multifunctional reservoir computing schemes to infer dynamical systems of different dimensions remains a challenge, and the feasibility of techniques such as padding for heterogeneous inputs needs further exploration.

**Why It Matters:** Generalizing MFRC to heterogeneous systems of varying dimensionality would significantly expand its applicability.

**Evidence:** Extending the proposed scheme to dynamical systems of different dimensions is another important issue that remains to be explored. One possible approach is to adopt the padding technique in data processing, yet the feasibility of this strategy remains to be investigated.
