title: Probabilistic Building Load Forecasting
---

**Background:** Building load forecasting often utilizes deterministic point prediction, which provides a single future load value without quantifying the potential range of fluctuation or reliability.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should expand current deterministic building load forecasting architectures into probabilistic models capable of quantifying predictive uncertainty to better inform risk-aware energy management and grid stability.

**Why It Matters:** Probabilistic forecasting provides critical insights into the volatility of building loads, which is essential for effective demand response and reserve market participation.
