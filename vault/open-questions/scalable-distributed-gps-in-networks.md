title: Scalable distributed GP inference
---

**Background:** Distributed Gaussian process inference in multi-agent systems often faces significant communication constraints when coordinating agents to maintain coherent global beliefs.

**Question / Future Work:** Scalable, communication-efficient protocols for decentralized Gaussian process inference are required to support large-scale sensor networks. Research into consensus-based filtering and distributed variational inference is needed to address performance bottlenecks under strict bandwidth or energy limitations.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for enabling scalable, fault-tolerant, and autonomous sensor networks where decentralized signal estimation is required.

**Evidence:** Another open direction concerns scalability in multi-agent networks, where thousands of sensors or robots must coordinate their inference under communication constraints.
