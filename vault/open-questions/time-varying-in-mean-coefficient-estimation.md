title: Time-varying In-mean Coefficients
---

**Background:** The influence of latent volatility on conditional means is often treated with static coefficients in high-dimensional forecasting models.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating methods to allow in-mean coefficients to be time-varying, potentially reflecting shifts in monetary policy or structural economic regimes.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the temporal stability of the risk-return trade-off is critical for structural inference and more robust long-horizon forecasting.
