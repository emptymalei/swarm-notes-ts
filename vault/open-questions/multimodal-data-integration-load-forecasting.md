title: Integrating Heterogeneous Building Data
---

**Background:** Building energy consumption patterns are deeply influenced by occupant behaviors, spatial configurations, and real-time operational schedules.

**Question / Future Work:** Future developments in load forecasting frameworks should prioritize the integration of multi-source heterogeneous data, specifically real-time occupancy schedules, user behavioral patterns, and spatial building topologies, to enhance the fidelity of models in complex scenarios.

**Why It Matters:** Accounting for the underlying drivers of building energy usage improves model explainability and performance, especially in environments with high occupancy-driven volatility.

**Evidence:** Future research will focus on integrating broader multi-source data, such as real-time occupancy schedules, user behaviors, and spatial building topologies, to better model complex load dynamics.
