title: Spatiotemporal graph outage dynamics
---

**Background:** Extreme weather data in outage prediction models is often provided as static snapshots, which ignores the temporal evolution of storm events.

**Question / Future Work:** Existing models often represent extreme weather events as static snapshots of meteorological and infrastructure variables, failing to capture the propagation of storms and cascading outage dynamics over time. Research into spatio-temporal graph formulations where both node states and edge connections evolve continuously could better model the progression of weather events.

**Why It Matters:** Modeling the dynamic, time-dependent nature of storms is critical for accurate, real-time outage forecasting.
