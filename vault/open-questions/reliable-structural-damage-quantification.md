title: Reliable structural damage quantification
---

**Background:** Vibration-based damage monitoring often produces latent space anomalies that lack a clear, physically interpretable mapping to quantitative damage metrics.

**Question / Future Work:** Research into establishing robust mappings between learned latent representations and quantitative physical degradation, potentially by integrating physics-informed constraints or improved scoring metrics.

**Why It Matters:** Damage quantification is necessary for transitioning from qualitative detection to actionable, decision-oriented maintenance planning.
