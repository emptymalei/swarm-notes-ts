title: Robust EFT Model Compression
---

**Background:** The compression of the EFT model parameter space using Fisher matrix analysis typically relies on a fiducial choice of parameters, potentially leading to a loss of accuracy if the true parameters diverge from the fiducial model.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is needed to generalize compression techniques for EFT parameters to account for their functional dependence on the linear bias, ensuring that the compression space remains valid and robust when data deviates from fiducial simulation-based priors.

**Why It Matters:** Ensuring the robustness of the compression technique across a wider range of parameter space is essential for reliable cosmological inference using high-precision survey data.

**Evidence:** orthonormality, as well as compression, may deteriorate in real-data applications when the truth diverges from our fiducial choice of b_1(z). The one immediate solution to this is to parameterize the functional dependence of compression vectors, q_n = q_n(b_1)... We leave this to future work.
