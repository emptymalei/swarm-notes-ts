title: Multi-dimensional Rubric Optimization
---

**Background:** Automated research systems often prioritize output performance metrics while lacking mechanisms to evaluate the structural and semantic integrity of the scientific replication process.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research needs to incorporate multi-dimensional evaluation rubrics—going beyond mere result-centric metrics—to guide agents in achieving higher-fidelity replication and scientifically grounded architectural innovations.

**Why It Matters:** This is critical for ensuring that automated research discovery leads to meaningful scientific advancement rather than overfitting to performance benchmarks.

**Evidence:** In our current realization of AutoSOTA pipeline, the evaluation and refinement loops have primarily adopted a results-centric strategy... Future work will expand the utility of AgentObjective by incorporating the remaining dimensions of the tree-structured rubric into the replication and optimization cycle.
