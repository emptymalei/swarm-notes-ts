title: Cross-Platform SXR Synthesis Robustness
---

**Background:** Cross-platform deployment of SXR synthesis models is often hindered by variations in available EUV spectral channels across different space-based solar observatories.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating the robustness and performance of SXR synthesis models when subjected to restricted or heterogeneous channel inputs from non-Sun-Earth-line missions is essential for extending flare monitoring capabilities to a wider heliospheric network.

**Why It Matters:** This is a prerequisite for creating a unified 'virtual GOES' capability across disparate international solar space missions.

**Evidence:** In the future, we plan to perform a full retraining of the model using the STEREO wavelengths to get a better understanding of the limitations we observe here.
