title: Formal convergence of SBBTS training
---

**Background:** The Schrödinger-Bass Bridge (SBB) framework provides a method for joint optimization of drift and volatility in diffusion processes, but theoretical guarantees for the convergence of training algorithms remain unproven.

**Question / Future Work:** Although the proposed neural training algorithm for the SBBTS framework shows consistent empirical convergence in practice, formal mathematical proofs ensuring its convergence to the optimal solution are currently lacking. Establishing these rigorous guarantees is essential for the theoretical foundation of the method.

**Why It Matters:** Rigorous convergence proofs are necessary to validate the stability and reliability of the training process, especially when applied to complex, high-dimensional financial data where empirical success alone is insufficient for robust deployment.
