title: Interpretability of topological reading features
---

**Background:** Topological data analysis provides high-dimensional descriptors that are often difficult to map directly to interpretable behavioral or cognitive processes.

**Question / Future Work:** Establish a formal mapping between topological descriptors (such as persistence diagrams or images) and behavioral markers in eye-tracking, such as regressive movements or fluency, to satisfy clinical requirements for interpretable diagnostics.

**Why It Matters:** Linking topological descriptors to cognitive theories is a prerequisite for clinical adoption of automated dyslexia screening tools.

**Evidence:** ...and on interpreting the topological features to better connect them to cognitive theories of reading and clinical practice.
