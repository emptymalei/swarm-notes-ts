title: ToE grounding in complex fusions
---

**Background:** Multimodal model architectures increasingly rely on complex integration strategies like cross-attention and early fusion, which complicate the identification of discrete, interpretable evidence sources.

**Question / Future Work:** The current interpretability framework for discrete evidence identification, such as ToE, is primarily validated on late-fusion architectures. Extending these search-based grounding methods to tightly coupled architectures remains a significant unresolved challenge.

**Why It Matters:** As LMMs shift towards deeper cross-modal interaction, maintaining auditable grounding is essential for deployment in high-stakes domains.
