**Background:** The study analyzed participant failures across image, tabular, and time-series data modalities, each having unique structural properties.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should focus on designing specific fault-tolerance methods that leverage the insights from the identified failure-impact modifiers to adaptively increase the dependability of FL systems in cross-silo environments.

**Why It Matters:** The ultimate goal of failure analysis is to translate observations into actionable, robust system designs, which requires developing concrete, adaptive fault-tolerance mechanisms based on the derived modifier knowledge.

**Evidence:** As future work, we plan to design fault-tolerance methods that use these insights to increase the dependability of FL systems.
