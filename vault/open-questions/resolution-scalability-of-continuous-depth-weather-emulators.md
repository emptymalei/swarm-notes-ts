title: Resolution Scalability of Weather Emulators
---

**Background:** Neural network-based weather emulators frequently face computational constraints that necessitate training at coarse spatial resolutions.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains unclear how the predictive dynamics learned by continuous-depth Neural ODE models scale as spatial resolution increases, specifically whether the physical consistency provided by soft constraints holds or degrades at higher densities.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding resolution-scalability is critical for transitioning data-driven emulators from research experiments to operational, high-fidelity forecasting systems.
