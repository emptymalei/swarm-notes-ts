title: Informative Missingness in Multimodal Dynamics
---

**Background:** Multimodal electronic health records contain both structured clinical data and unstructured clinical text, which are recorded through potentially different and non-random processes that depend on latent patient states.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open research problem to effectively integrate and leverage informative missingness across time for diverse modalities to learn robust patient representations for sequential clinical decision-making.

**Why It Matters:** This is critical for clinical reinforcement learning, as patient state representations that fail to account for the endogenous observation processes may lead to biased policies.
