title: Multivariate and Causal Graph Entropy
---

**Background:** Sample entropy metrics for graph signals rely on specific topology-aware embeddings to quantify signal irregularity.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the optimal embedding strategy and extending entropy frameworks to multivariate and causal settings (e.g., multivariate SampEn, transfer entropy) remains a key bottleneck for characterizing complex graph signal dynamics.

**Why It Matters:** The development of robust multivariate and causal extensions is essential for moving beyond simple univariate signal analysis to understanding interdependent network systems.
