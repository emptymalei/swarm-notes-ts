title: Coupling internal and external risk assessment
---

**Background:** Autonomous navigation systems must increasingly process both internal system health data and external environmental perception simultaneously to ensure safety.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing architectures that jointly integrate internal geometric consistency measures with external environmental perception remains an open challenge for end-to-end risk-aware autonomy.

**Why It Matters:** Unifying internal and external risk assessment is necessary for autonomous systems operating in unknown, unstructured environments where both system integrity and situational awareness are critical.
