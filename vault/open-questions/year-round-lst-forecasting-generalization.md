title: Year-round LST forecasting generalization
---

**Background:** Land Surface Temperature (LST) forecasting models typically struggle to maintain performance across different seasons due to variations in physical drivers such as solar forcing and land-atmosphere interactions.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the extension of spatiotemporal downscaling and nowcasting frameworks to include the colder seasons, which requires managing seasonal shifts in environmental and physical controls. This is essential for achieving robust, year-round operational LST monitoring.

**Why It Matters:** The limitation to warm-season training prevents models from being used for year-round climate applications or monitoring heat-related dynamics in non-summer periods.
