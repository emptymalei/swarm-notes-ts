title: Data-Driven CME Modeling
---

**Background:** Data-driven solar physics models increasingly aim to use direct observational data to initialize and drive simulations of active region evolution.

**Question / Future Work:** Transitioning from idealized boundary conditions to driving magnetohydrodynamic models with high-resolution, time-dependent vector magnetograms requires robust methods for assimilating observational data while ensuring numerical stability and consistency of the global model.

**Why It Matters:** Data-driven modeling is essential for moving from theoretical studies to reliable operational space weather forecasting that reflects actual solar conditions.
