title: Causal Alignment in Driving Planning
---

**Background:** World models for autonomous driving often rely on loosely coupled planning, which can lead to inconsistencies between future visual scene generation and vehicle trajectory execution.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing robust causal alignment between generative visual forecasting and action-sequence planning remains a primary bottleneck for achieving consistent long-horizon closed-loop driving performance across heterogeneous sensor domains.

**Why It Matters:** This represents a fundamental challenge in unifying perception and control within generative architectures, impacting the reliability and safety of world-model-based autonomous driving.
