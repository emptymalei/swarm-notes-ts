title: Inner rim substructure mechanisms
---

**Background:** Post-AGB circumbinary discs frequently exhibit inner rim substructures at the 1-10 AU scale, yet their formation mechanism remains debated.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the physical origin of azimuthal brightness enhancements in circumbinary inner rims to distinguish between thermal effects, binary-induced dynamical responses, and hydrodynamical instabilities like vortices. Determining if these are transient or persistent is essential for interpreting the dynamical evolution of these systems.

**Why It Matters:** Identifying the physical drivers of these morphologies is necessary for understanding the interplay between central binary systems and their surrounding disc evolution.
