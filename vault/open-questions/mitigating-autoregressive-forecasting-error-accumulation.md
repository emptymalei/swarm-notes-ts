title: Mitigating Autoregressive Forecasting Error Accumulation
---

**Background:** Autoregressive weather forecasting models are prone to cumulative errors that degrade predictions over longer lead times.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating mechanisms to incorporate temporal consistency constraints or diffusion-based generative processes to mitigate error accumulation in long-horizon autoregressive forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** Cumulative error remains the primary bottleneck for extending lead-time accuracy in AI-based atmospheric and spatiotemporal prediction.

**Evidence:** GSSA-ViT remains susceptible to error accumulation over extended forecast horizons, a common challenge for autoregressive AI weather models.
