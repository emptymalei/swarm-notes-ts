title: DeFi Event-Aware Forecasting Microstructure
---

**Background:** Automated Market Makers (AMMs) drive on-chain asset pricing through deterministic reserve ratio mechanisms triggered by discrete, event-driven transactions. Forecasting these event sequences remains challenging due to the heavy-tailed, irregular nature of inter-arrival times and the interdependent, multi-modal nature of protocol events.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing robust forecasting models that capture the micro-temporal evolution of liquidity pool states across diverse decentralized finance (DeFi) protocols is an open challenge. Specifically, effectively joint-modeling discrete event types with irregular, bursty block intervals requires architectures that go beyond traditional temporal point processes to handle high-variance on-chain dynamics.

**Why It Matters:** This is critical for advancing automated yield speculation, portfolio rebalancing, and real-time arbitrage execution in DeFi, where timing and type accuracy are essential.
