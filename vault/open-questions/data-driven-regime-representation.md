title: Data-Driven Regime Representation Learning
---

**Background:** Time-series forecasting models often rely on predefined indicators to characterize shifts in environment or regime.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating whether latent, end-to-end learned state representations can surpass manual or heuristic regime-characterization methods for model selection and routing in non-stationary environments.

**Why It Matters:** Manual regime definition is prone to misspecification and limits adaptability in complex, high-dimensional forecasting tasks.

**Evidence:** Research is needed to determine if machine-learned or latent data-driven market-state representations could provide superior sensitivity to regime changes.
