title: Multi-periodic LDP Reconstruction Limits
---

**Background:** Local differential privacy (LDP) noise often masks sub-dominant periodic components in time-series data, effectively limiting reconstruction to the most prominent signal rhythm.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to extend privacy-preserving signal reconstruction to identify and recover multiple, heterogeneous periodicities within a single stream, especially when weaker periodic signals are indistinguishable from injected LDP noise.

**Why It Matters:** Most complex multimedia and physiological signals contain multiple overlapping periodic rhythms; limiting reconstruction to a single period severely restricts the fidelity and utility of privacy-preserving systems.
