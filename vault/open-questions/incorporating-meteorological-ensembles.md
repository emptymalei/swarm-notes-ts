title: Incorporating meteorological ensembles
---

**Background:** Scenario generation for electricity price path forecasting is often restricted to internal data sources and neglects external meteorologically-driven ensemble forecasts.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should aim to enrich the scenario generation process by incorporating external meteorological ensemble forecasts to enhance the richness and predictive power of the uncertainty representation.

**Why It Matters:** Capturing the weather-dependent stochastic nature of electricity supply and demand is essential for improving forecast accuracy.

**Evidence:** the scenario generation approach is deliberately restricted to standard data sources and does not incorporate external meteorological ensemble forecasts.
