title: Hardware-Aware Pruning Bottlenecks
---

**Background:** Pruning strategies that are agnostic to the underlying hardware often fail to achieve optimal energy-efficiency gains in practical time series classification deployments.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating how to optimize model pruning processes by explicitly conditioning them on hardware-specific constraints and architectural profiles remains an open bottleneck for efficient machine learning.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential for moving toward truly sustainable and resource-aware machine learning deployments.

**Evidence:** Our work could be further extended toward... explicitly considering the hardware at hand for pruning, connecting to hardware-aware optimization.
