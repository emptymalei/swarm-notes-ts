title: Automated Gate Regularization Selection
---

**Background:** Gated Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KANs) use regularized gating mechanisms to perform sparse nonlinear corrections on linear forecasting baselines, but selecting optimal sparsity hyperparameters remains a heuristic process.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing automated methods for selecting gate regularization parameters is necessary to optimize the trade-off between model sparsity, nonlinear utilization, and forecast accuracy across diverse datasets.

**Why It Matters:** The lack of an automated selection mechanism limits the robustness and ease of deployment of gated KAN architectures.
