title: Robust Inference for Survey Data
---

**Background:** Simulation-based inference (SBI) methods, such as Neural Ratio Estimators (NREs), typically rely on the assumption of perfect knowledge of the data-generating process, which is often an idealization in real-world astrophysical observations.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to transition simulation-based forecasting to real-world application by addressing model misspecification, systematic observational errors, and unknown data-generating processes that currently limit the deployment of NREs on actual survey data.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the simulation-to-reality gap is the primary bottleneck for adopting advanced SBI techniques in large-scale observational astronomy.
