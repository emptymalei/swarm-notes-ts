title: Refining Hybrid TSC Integration Strategies
---

**Background:** Hybrid classifiers for time series classification frequently combine diverse feature transformation methods, such as those derived from kernel-based and interval-based strategies, to improve predictive performance.

**Question / Future Work:** Methods for integrating disparate feature transformation sets—specifically for hybrid models combining Hydra and Quant—remain suboptimal, as simple concatenation followed by existing classification logic does not fully realize the potential gains in predictive quality or computational efficiency. More advanced, theoretically grounded integration strategies are required to effectively merge these complementary feature spaces.

**Why It Matters:** The current lack of optimal integration methods for hybrid time series classifiers prevents the full realization of the performance benefits offered by merging complementary feature extraction paradigms.
