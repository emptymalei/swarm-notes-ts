title: Resource-Aware Online Adaptation Constraints
---

**Background:** Online learning frameworks for industrial systems face challenges in balancing adaptation responsiveness with the computational constraints of real-time edge deployment.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating how to perform continuous online model updates while strictly adhering to the memory, latency, and power constraints of edge-deployed industrial controllers is a significant unresolved challenge.

**Why It Matters:** The computational burden of continuous fine-tuning is a primary barrier to deploying adaptive models in distributed, real-time industrial environments.

**Evidence:** Second, we will develop model compression and resource-aware online updating for edge deployment, reducing fine-tuning cost while maintaining reliable drift responsiveness toward real-time closed-loop applications.
