title: Model-independent BAO distance extraction
---

**Background:** Baryon acoustic oscillation (BAO) measurements rely on fiducial cosmological models to extract distance information from galaxy clustering data, potentially introducing model dependence.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop and refine model-independent methodologies to extract cosmological distances without assuming an underlying dark energy fluid or specific spatial curvature. Current BAO analysis techniques often use phenomenological templates that may be biased when applied to non-concordance models, requiring further validation of these geometric techniques to ensure they serve as truly unbiased probes of expansion history.

**Why It Matters:** The reliance of current BAO measurements on fiducial models limits their ability to independently test alternative dark energy scenarios, potentially obscuring evidence for non-standard physics.
