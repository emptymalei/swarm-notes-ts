title: Fusion Forecasting Generalization Bottlenecks
---

**Background:** Neural network-based forecasting of critical plasma events in tokamaks is hindered by data scarcity and high variability in discharge conditions across different experimental runs.

**Question / Future Work:** Establish rigorous methodologies to quantify model performance on limited-data fusion plasma datasets and develop techniques to improve generalization across diverse operational regimes and device configurations.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization is the central bottleneck for deploying ML-based safety and control systems in fusion devices, where labeled data for critical failure modes is inherently sparse.
