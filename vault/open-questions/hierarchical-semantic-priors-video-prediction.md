title: Advancing Hierarchical Semantic Priors
---

**Background:** Generative video prediction models often struggle with temporal consistency and scene semantics, as latent representations frequently conflate high-level structure with transient visual details. Hierarchical frameworks address this by decoupling semantic representation forecasting from pixel-level synthesis.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open question how to best integrate diverse, multi-modal semantic priors—such as explicit 3D geometry or scene graphs beyond implicit vision foundation model features—into hierarchical video prediction pipelines. Resolving this is essential for enhancing the structural reasoning and multi-modal consistency of world models in complex, dynamic environments.

**Why It Matters:** This direction is critical for moving beyond current VFM feature representations towards more physically grounded and controllable world modeling.
