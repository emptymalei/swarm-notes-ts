title: Scalability of Agentic Forecasting
---

**Background:** Agentic forecasting systems frequently rely on iterative LLM reasoning and search, but scaling these systems to broader forecasting tasks beyond binary outcomes remains challenging.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to extend agentic forecasting to categorical and numerical outcome prediction, to develop better integration of market metadata for weighting signals, and to explore continual learning or reinforcement learning for agentic meta-controllers.

**Why It Matters:** Binary forecasting is a limited scope; general-purpose forecasting agents must be able to handle continuous variables and multiclass categorization to be useful in real-world scenarios.
