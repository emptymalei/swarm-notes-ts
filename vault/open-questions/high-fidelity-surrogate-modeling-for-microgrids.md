title: High-fidelity surrogate modeling for microgrids
---

**Background:** Renewable energy systems frequently exhibit non-linear dynamics, such as battery degradation and complex PV efficiency curves, that are often simplified in existing simulation frameworks.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop and integrate more sophisticated surrogate models for lithium-ion batteries and PV generation into simulation environments to better capture non-linearities and long-term degradation effects.

**Why It Matters:** Improving the fidelity of physics-based models is essential for bridging the gap between digital twins and real-world microgrid operational behavior, which is critical for reliable control strategy validation.

**Evidence:** Third, more sophisticated surrogate models for lithium-ion batteries and PV generation will be added, enabling higher-fidelity simulations that capture nonlinearities and degradation effects.
