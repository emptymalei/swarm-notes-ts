title: Transformer Extrapolation Failure
---

**Background:** Transformers often rely on permutation-invariant attention mechanisms that may hinder their ability to learn how the structural characteristics of temporal dynamics evolve as system parameters change.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating why standard Transformer architectures fail to generalize to unseen parameter regimes in nonlinear dynamical systems, specifically regarding their inability to predict catastrophic collapse or state transitions, compared to reservoir computing models that inherently mimic dynamical systems.

**Why It Matters:** This question addresses a fundamental limitation of current foundation models for physical dynamics and scientific discovery, highlighting the trade-off between architectural flexibility and inductive physical priors.
