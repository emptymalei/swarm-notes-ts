title: 3D Geometric Re-projection Navigation
---

**Background:** Navigating complex, three-dimensional unstructured environments presents challenges for existing geometric re-projection methods originally designed for simpler 2D navigation tasks.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research aims to extend the current analytical geometric re-projection mechanism, which relies on SE(2) transformations, to effectively handle navigation in 3D unstructured environments where robotic motion and obstacle geometries are more complex.

**Why It Matters:** This is crucial for scaling the framework to mobile robots operating in diverse and non-planar real-world terrains.
