title: Multivariate Target Benchmarking
---

**Background:** Tabular foundation models are increasingly capable of generating multivariate outputs, requiring benchmarks to extend beyond single-target regression.

**Question / Future Work:** Expanding evaluation protocols to support multivariate (joint) targets is necessary to assess the quality of inter-variable dependencies predicted by tabular foundation models, moving beyond current univariate-only assessment frameworks.

**Why It Matters:** Many real-world tabular prediction tasks involve predicting multiple interrelated variables simultaneously; benchmarking these capabilities is essential for assessing the full utility of foundation models.
