title: Joint Multi-Step Prediction Coverage
---

**Background:** SCDR provides asymptotic coverage guarantees for one-step-ahead prediction regions in time-series data. Current developments primarily address individual prediction sets.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of methodologies that provide joint coverage guarantees for prediction regions across multiple future time steps (H-step ahead prediction) is required for path-dependent forecasting applications.

**Why It Matters:** Joint coverage is essential for many practical applications where the entire trajectory or sequence of future states is of interest, rather than only individual time points.

**Evidence:** we believe certain problems benefit from a coverage guarantee on the joint prediction sets over H steps.
