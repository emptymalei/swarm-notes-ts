title: Explicit Shift-Aware Anomaly Detection
---

**Background:** Industrial monitoring systems frequently encounter distribution shifts due to evolving system conditions, requiring models to adapt beyond simple periodic retraining.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing mechanisms to make anomaly detection models explicitly shift-aware—such as through residual-distribution monitoring and adaptive thresholding—remains a critical challenge for long-term operational resilience in multivariate time series.

**Why It Matters:** Industrial anomaly detection systems must be robust to non-stationary environments; addressing distribution shifts directly is necessary to maintain high precision and low false-positive rates over time.
