title: Scaling Neural Markov Models
---

**Background:** Markov transition operators provide an interpretable framework for modeling non-stationary stochastic systems, but empirical estimation often fails due to data sparsity in high-resolution settings.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending neural-parameterized Markov frameworks from single-variable, low-frequency settings to high-frequency multi-asset universes remains an essential challenge for leveraging cross-sectional correlation dynamics while maintaining interpretability.

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond single-asset analysis is crucial for validating the scalability of this approach and its practical utility in real-world systematic financial management.
