title: Assessing VLM Financial Chart Reasoning
---

**Background:** Vision-Language Models (VLMs) have shown potential in financial forecasting using candlestick charts, but their ability to identify specific technical indicators such as support/resistance levels and classic chart patterns remains largely unexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop standardized, task-specific evaluation datasets that include semantic annotations for financial chart concepts like resistance levels, support levels, and specific chart patterns to assess whether VLMs are truly interpreting these visual elements rather than relying on statistical heuristics.

**Why It Matters:** Validating the fundamental technical reasoning capabilities of VLMs in finance is critical for moving beyond 'black box' performance metrics toward robust, interpretable financial decision-support systems.
