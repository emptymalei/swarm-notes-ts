title: Predictability mechanism around announcements
---

**Background:** The predictive power of skewness dispersion for aggregate stock market returns is concentrated around major macroeconomic announcements, particularly monetary policy events.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains unclear whether the predictability around macroeconomic announcements is primarily driven by the resolution of aggregate risk or by the elimination of mispricing resulting from gradual information incorporation. Further research is required to decouple these two mechanisms and to determine if similar predictive patterns emerge around other significant macroeconomic data releases.

**Why It Matters:** Distinguishing between rational risk-based channels and behavioral mispricing channels is a fundamental challenge in financial economics with implications for market efficiency and policy analysis.
