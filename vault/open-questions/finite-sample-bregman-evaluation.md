title: Finite-sample Bregman evaluation
---

**Background:** The theoretical evaluation of point predictors under the decision-theoretic framework of mean-consistent loss functions involves the decomposition of expected loss into miscalibration and discrimination components.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the necessary properties of Bregman divergences for robust finite-sample model validation within the framework of Murphy's decomposition, specifically focusing on mitigating biases introduced by predictor-dependent weighting in heuristic accuracy measures.

**Why It Matters:** Addresses the critical gap between theoretically sound scoring rules and commonly used, yet potentially dishonest, empirical accuracy metrics.

**Evidence:** This may give raise to consider different necessary properties of L for finite sample validation. This is an open research topic.
