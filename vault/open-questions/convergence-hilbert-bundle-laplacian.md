title: Convergence for Infinite-Dimensional Fibers
---

**Background:** Hilbert bundles consist of infinite-dimensional fibers indexed over an irregular domain, requiring new convolutional learning frameworks to replace traditional finite-dimensional graph or manifold approaches.

**Question / Future Work:** Generalizing convergence results for sheaf Laplacians to Hilbert bundles with infinite-dimensional fibers is critical for consistent learning theories on complex data like time series or operators. Future work is needed to extend these convergence results beyond current assumptions of compact manifolds and specific sampling distributions to broader, more realistic geometric settings.

**Why It Matters:** This question is fundamental to unifying geometric deep learning theory and establishing consistent convergence for infinite-dimensional data modalities on non-Euclidean domains.

**Evidence:** the lack of an extension of the convergence result in [14] to bundles with infinite-dimensional fibers
