title: Standalone Mamba TSC Capacity
---

**Background:** State space models (SSMs) like Mamba have shown significant performance in time series forecasting, yet their standalone capacity for time series classification (TSC) remains underexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to isolate and evaluate the intrinsic performance of Mamba-based architectures on TSC tasks without reliance on external feature engineering techniques (e.g., ROCKET or wavelets) that complicate credit assignment.

**Why It Matters:** It is crucial to determine if SSMs are viable backbones on their own merits rather than through hybrid feature-engineering pipelines.
