title: Hierarchical Latent Dynamics for DLOs
---

**Background:** The modeling of deformable linear object (DLO) dynamics involves a trade-off between capturing fine-grained local state details and maintaining global structural integrity over long temporal horizons.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to develop hierarchical latent architectures that bridge the performance gap between accurate short-term local reconstruction and stable long-term predictive dynamics, and to address the sim-to-real gap through online system identification.

**Why It Matters:** This represents a critical bottleneck for real-world deployment of DLO dynamics models, as the current performance relies on simulation-trained latent spaces that may not generalize to diverse material parameters.
