title: Cross-region Meteorology Forecast Generalization
---

**Background:** Meteorology-aware forecasting frameworks are currently evaluated within specific convective regimes, often limiting their applicability to diverse orographic and climatic contexts.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open research challenge to determine the cross-regional generalizability of meteorology-informed spatiotemporal architectures, specifically across different orographic and climatic regions.

**Why It Matters:** Evaluating cross-region generalizability is vital for deploying nowcasting models in diverse geographic environments where convective dynamics vary significantly.

**Evidence:** Generalizability to other orographic and climatic regions remains to be tested, as the current evaluation is limited to a convective regime in eastern China.
