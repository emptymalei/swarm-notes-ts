title: Integrating Geodynamic Variability into PN Models
---

**Background:** The accuracy of Earth Orientation Parameters (EOP) and Celestial Pole Offsets (CPO) prediction is constrained by the assumption of fixed geodynamic parameters in standard Precession-Nutation (PN) models.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to determine if integrating time-varying geophysical parameters (such as the second-degree gravitational harmonic coefficient J2) into PN models can reduce the residual errors in current EOP series and improve the baseline reliability for long-term CPO forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a fundamental modeling deficiency that limits current forecasting performance regardless of the statistical algorithm used.
