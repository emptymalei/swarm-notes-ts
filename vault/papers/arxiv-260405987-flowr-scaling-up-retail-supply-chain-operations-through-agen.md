# Flowr -- Scaling Up Retail Supply Chain Operations Through Agentic AI in Large Scale Supermarket Chains

**Authors**: Eranga Bandara, Ross Gore, Sachin Shetty, Piumi Siyambalapitiya, Sachini Rajapakse, Isurunima Kularathna, Pramoda Karunarathna, Ravi Mukkamala, Peter Foytik, Safdar H. **Date**: 2026-04-07
**Paper ID**: [arxiv:2604.05987](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05987)

## Summary

Flowr is an agentic AI framework designed to automate fragmented and manual retail supply chain operations, such as demand forecasting and procurement, within large supermarket chains. The framework utilizes a consortium of domain-specialized LLMs coordinated by a central reasoning agent to handle decision-intensive tasks. By incorporating a human-in-the-loop orchestration model via the Model Context Protocol, Flowr ensures that managers can maintain oversight and accountability while scaling operations effectively. Empirical results from a large-scale supermarket chain indicate that the framework significantly reduces manual coordination effort and improves demand-supply alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces Flowr, a novel agentic AI framework that decomposes manual supply chain workflows into specialized, LLM-driven cognitive agents.
- Implements a central reasoning LLM orchestration layer to coordinate domain-specialized LLMs for end-to-end task automation.
- Integrates a Model Context Protocol (MCP)-enabled interface for human-in-the-loop oversight to ensure accountability and control in high-stakes operational environments.
