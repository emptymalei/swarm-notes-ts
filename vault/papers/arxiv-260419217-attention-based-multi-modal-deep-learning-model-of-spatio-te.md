# Attention-based Multi-modal Deep Learning Model of Spatio-temporal Crop Yield Prediction with Satellite, Soil and Climate Data

**Authors**: Gopal Krishna Shyam, Ila Chandrakar
**Date**: 2026-04-21
**Paper ID**: [arxiv:2604.19217](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19217)

## Summary

This paper introduces the Attention-Based Multi-Modal Deep Learning Framework (ABMMDLF) for spatio-temporal crop yield prediction. The model integrates multi-modal data, including satellite imagery, high-resolution meteorological time-series, and soil properties, to overcome the limitations of traditional static forecasting methods. By utilizing a hybrid architecture of CNNs and a Temporal Attention Mechanism, the framework effectively captures complex spatio-temporal relationships and adapts to critical phenological changes, achieving an R^2 score of 0.89.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed the Attention-Based Multi-Modal Deep Learning Framework (ABMMDLF) for spatio-temporal crop yield prediction.
- Achieved an R^2 score of 0.89 by integrating multi-year satellite imagery, meteorological time-series, and static soil properties.
- Introduced a hybrid architecture combining CNNs for spatial feature extraction and a Temporal Attention Mechanism to prioritize critical phenological periods.
