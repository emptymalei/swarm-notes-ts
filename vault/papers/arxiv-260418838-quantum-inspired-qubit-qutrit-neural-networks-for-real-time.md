# Quantum inspired qubit qutrit neural networks for real time financial forecasting

**Authors**: Kanishk Bakshi, Kathiravan Srinivasan
**Date**: 2026-04-20
**Paper ID**: [arxiv:2604.18838](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.18838)

## Summary

This paper evaluates the performance of classical artificial neural networks, qubit-based neural networks (QQBNs), and a newly proposed quantum qutrit-based neural network (QQTN) for stock prediction. By comparing these architectures on quantitative financial metrics like the Sharpe ratio and Information Coefficient, the authors demonstrate that the QQTN provides superior accuracy and robustness while requiring less training time. These findings suggest that qutrit-based quantum-inspired architectures offer a viable path toward efficient, real-time financial market modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces Quantum Qutrit-based Neural Networks (QQTNs) for real-time financial forecasting.
- Demonstrates that QQTNs outperform classical ANNs and Qubit-based Neural Networks (QQBNs) in Sharpe ratio, Information Coefficient, and robustness.
- Shows that QQTNs achieve superior performance metrics compared to classical counterparts while significantly reducing required training time.
