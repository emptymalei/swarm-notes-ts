# VLTI/PIONIER imaging of post-AGB binaries. An INSPIRING hunt for inner rim substructures in circumbinary discs

**Authors**: Toon De Prins, Akke Corporaal, Jacques Kluska, Devika Kamath, Hans Van Winckel, Kateryna Andrych, Javier Alcolea, Narsireddy Anugu, Jean-Philippe Berger, Valentin Bujarrabal, Ivan Gallardo Cava, Stefan Kraus, Hans Olofsson
**Date**: 2026-05-13
**Paper ID**: [arxiv:2605.13445](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13445)

## Summary

This study presents a high-resolution near-infrared interferometric imaging survey of eight post-AGB circumbinary discs, utilizing VLTI/PIONIER data to resolve inner rim structures at the 1-10 AU scale. By employing an image reconstruction workflow that integrates the SPARCO approach with the ORGANIC algorithm, the authors recover high-fidelity images of dust continuum emission. The results reveal significant structural diversity, with several targets exhibiting azimuthal brightness enhancements or complex arc-like features that cannot be explained solely by disc inclination, indicating active dynamical processes within these environments.

## Key Contributions

- Presents the first homogeneous interferometric imaging survey of the inner regions of eight post-AGB circumbinary discs using VLTI/PIONIER.
- Reveals widespread morphological diversity and azimuthal brightness enhancements in inner discs at 1-10 AU scales, suggesting complex dynamical interactions.
- Demonstrates an image reconstruction pipeline combining SPARCO and ORGANIC to reliably recover structural features from sparse interferometric data.
